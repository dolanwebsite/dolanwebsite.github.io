Tory Johnson Biography

Tory Johnson is a Workplace Contributor on ABC’s Good Morning America, where she reaches millions of viewers on a wide range of job-related issues and challenges. She also anchors Home Work, a weekly program on ABC News Now, the digital channel. In addition to her television segments, Johnson is also a Business & Career Coach for AOL and a Career Expert for Yahoo! HotJobs. She is the founder and CEO of Women For Hire, which is the only producer of high caliber recruiting events for women.
