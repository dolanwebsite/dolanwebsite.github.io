Did Jesse Hogue Commit Suicide?

Jesse Hogue is said to have committed suicide based on hearsay while the reports are not yet verified.

Hogue is still alive and serving his sentence in Texas’ custody following the murder charges of the 1994 Cold Case.

There are various bogus news headlines on the web claiming Jesse’s death, even though there is no true evidence or authorized reports.

Likewise, law enforcement has not produced any police reports verifying his death. As a result, the speculation about his death is just an internet hoax.
