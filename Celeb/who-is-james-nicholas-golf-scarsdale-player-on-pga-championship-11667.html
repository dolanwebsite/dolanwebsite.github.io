Who Is James Nicholas Golf? Wikipedia Bio To Know

James Nicholas is a professional PGA Tour golfer from Scarsdale, New York.

Turned professional in 2019, he also won the Ivy League Player of the Year honors at Yale in the same year. He is currently competing in the AdventHealth Championship in Kansas City.

The 25-year-old is very passionate about the game and he has shown several unusual appearances for the sake of the game. One of such came yesterday when his flight was delayed and he left his backpack in the airport carousel to reach on time.
