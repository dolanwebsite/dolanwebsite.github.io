Tanya Charry is a Colombian Journalist, TV host, and researcher of entertainment news. After the recent announcement of her expected newborn, people are curious to know how old she is.

Tanya is best known for being the lead reporter and co-host of the highly rated Latino entertainment show El Gordo Y La Flaca, which translates as the Fat and the Skinny.

The show presents the latest entertainment news and interviews with famous actors, musicians, and celebrities.

How Old Is Tanya Charry? Age And Wikipedia

Born on December 30, 1974, Tanya is currently 47 years old. She was born in Barranquilla of Columbia and is based in Los Angeles, United States. Her zodiac sign is Capricorn.

Tanya’s mother has Alzheimer’s, but she always supports her daughter. Despite her illness, she attended Tanya’s wedding. Tanya also loves her mother and holds her very close.

Tanya has worked on TV shows like La Tijera or Scissors (2009) and El Gordo Y La Flaca or the Fat and the Skinny (2015). She has established her name as one of the most hardworking and passionate entertainment reporters.

In 2012, she traveled to Mexico to know the last details of the death of Jenni Rivera, achieving the complete chronology of the singer’s final moments.

Tanya is known to get up close and personal with the stars and get them to open up. She has interviewed or worked with top celebrities like Shakira, Farruko, Marc Anthony, Juan Gabriel, Antonio Banderas, etc.

Tanya Charry Is Expecting A Child With Her Husband Sebastian Jiménez

Tanya Charry has been married to Sebastián Jiménez since 2021. The duo wed at St. Hugh’s Church in Miami with around 150 guests on August 7, 2021.

Tanya is very close with 11-year-old Zoe and started bonding before marrying Sebastian.
