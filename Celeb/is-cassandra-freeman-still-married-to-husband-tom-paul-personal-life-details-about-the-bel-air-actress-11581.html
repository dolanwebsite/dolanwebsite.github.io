 Is Cassandra Freeman Still Married To Husband Tom Paul?

Cassandra Freeman is married to re-recording mixer and sound designer, Tom Paul. The couple who first shared their picture in 2014 is still together.

After dating for a few years, Cassandra tied the knot with Tom in 2018 in the attendance of friends and family. As they tend to live a low-key life, neither of them has shared any details regarding their big day.

According to her old posts, she was in a relationship with an anonymous man before meeting Paul. However, she hasn’t shared any details of her ex-boyfriend on social media.

Though Tom hasn’t shared his social media details, he often appears on Cassandra’s profile. The loving couple has been sharing their journey with their followers.

Cassandra Freeman Children Details

Cassandra Freeman welcomed a baby boy named Hudson Jelani Paul on the 25th of June 2018 with Tom Paul.

She has been sharing sweet moments of her baby growing up on her social media. Meanwhile, they have also been raising their child far from the limelight too.

Freeman has also mentioned herself as a mother to Hudson on her Instagram bio. At the time of her birth, she posted a picture of her with the newborn attaching with a heart-warming message.
