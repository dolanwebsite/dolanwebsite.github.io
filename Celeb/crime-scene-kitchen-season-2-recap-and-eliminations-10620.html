Crime Scene Kitchen Season 2 Recap and Eliminations

 Posted on November 10, 2023  |  8 minutes  |  1609 words  |  Noelle Montes

Crime Scene Kitchen Season 2 recap reveals the elimination of Steph & Cherry in episode 8. The show follows contests between two sets of chefs.

It’s a new season with all new surprises to challenge the skill of all new bakers. This time six classically trained bakers teams will go head-to-head with six self-taught teams.

There are two Crime Scene Kitchens, and they started with a safety bake, and whoever wins the first round automatically advances to the next stage of the competition.

Judges leave the hints behind in each round, and it’s up to the participant to sniff out the clues left behind.

Using the only clues left behind in this kitchen, they must figure out what was baked there and recreate the mystery desert as best they can in just two hours.

The only one who knows the identity of that mystery dessert is the judge’s youtube star Yolanda Gampp and Michelin-Starred chef Curtis Stone.

The right decision gets them closer to winning $100,000, and if they make the wrong move, they have to go home.

The first episode began with a self-taught baker and the next with a classically trained one. Halfway through the season, both teams will merge.

The show premiered on June 5, 2023.

Crime Scene Kitchen Season 2 Recap

Crime Scene Kitchen season 2 episode recap challenges self-taught and classically trained bakers. Episode 2 introduced 12 classically trained bakers.

In the first episode, 12 self-taught bakers investigated the Crime Scene in Kitchen.

The safety bake winners in episode 1 were Steph Hsu and Sin Yi Cherry Lau.

The mystery cake of the elimination round was an upside-down cake topped with caramel, oranges, and blood oranges.

The team who performed worst in the elimination bake was the stepbrothers Donovan Peters and Dayveion Shawver.

While self-taught bakers take a day off, classically trained teams got challenged in episode 2, Meet the Classically-Trained Competition.

Crime Scene Kitchen Season 2 Episode 2 Recap

Crime Scene Kitchen season 2 episode 2 recap reveals winners Camille & Laissa and eliminated Christina & Jenae.

They have two hours to recreate the Mystery bake where the teams made:

  • Jane & Christina: 6 Maple Bacon Donut Bars
  • Camille & Laissa: 2 Strawberry Donuts, 2 Raspberry Donuts, and 2 Chocolate Glazed Donuts
  • Sherry & Sally: 2 Strawbery Donuts, 2 Maple Bacon Donuts & 2 Blueberry Donuts
  • Yass & Amber: 2 Rasberry Donuts, 2 Blueberry Donuts & 2 Maple Bacon Chocolate Donuts
  • Dj & Ricky: 3 Maple Bacon Donuts & 3 Mixed Berry Donuts
  • Bob & Vikki: 6 Maple & Bacon Donuts filled with berry jam

The mystery dessert of the safety bake turned out to be two strawberry jelly-filled sugar donuts, two chocolate, and two pink donuts.

Camille & Laissa won the safety bake challenge, skipped the elimination bake, and automatically advanced in the competition.

Curtis decided on the elimination bake, and each team got two minutes to examine the clue.

  • Amber & Yass: Coca macaron cake with whipped cream & Berries
  • Sherry & Sally: Pavlova with whipped cream & berries
  • Bob & Vikki: Dacquoise with whipped cream & berries
  • Jeane & Christina: Three-layer dacquoise with whipped cream & berries
  • Ricky & DJ: Three-layer pavlova with strawberries, blueberries, and raspberries
  • Mystery cake: Pavlova cake with whipped cream and fresh berries

Three of the dessert missed the mark, multiple desserts were incorrect, and they judged half of them from mystery dessert.

Christina Mathis & Jenae Cartwright who went home in Crime Scene Kitchen episode 2.

Crime Scene Kitchen Season 2 Episode 3 Recap

Crime Scene Kitchen season 2 episode 3 recap reveals winner Steph & Cherry and eliminated chefs Michelle and Torre.

In the safety bake competition of the episode Self-Taught: Not So Simple, the teams made the following desserts:

  • T & Fadi: Double-layer brownie with a mint cream cheese frosting
  • Kathleen & Hannah: cream cheese mint brownies & white chocolate blondies
  • Torre & Michelle: Mint cream cheese brownies without caramel
  • Steph & Cherry: Coffee streusel & cream de menthe brownies
  • Trasha & Kristy: Mint cheesecake & caramel pecan turtle brownies

The mystery dessert was disclosed as two types of fudge brownies: mint chocolate brownies with mint cream frosting and chocolate topping and coffee brownies.

The winners of the safety bake were Steph & Cherry.

In the elimination bake challenge, the teams prepared the following dessert:

  • Kathleen & Hannah: Cheesecake with a graham cracker crust
  • Torre & Michelle: Cheesecake with a chocolate graham cracker crust
  • T & Fadi: crustless cheesecake
  • Trasha & Kristy: basque cheesecake

The host revealed the mystery cake as a basque cheesecake accompanied by blueberries and blackberries.

Michelle and Torre are the bakers who got eliminated from season 2, episode 3, Self-Taught: Not So Simple.

Crime Scene Kitchen Season 2 Episode 4 Recap

Crime Scene Kitchen season 2 episode 4 recap revealed the winners, Amber & Yass, and eliminated Bob & Vikki.

Five classically trained bakers return to solve culinary crimes in the kitchen.

In the safety bake competition, the mystery dessert was clafoutis with raspberries dusted with powdered sugar.

Amber Croom and Yasmeen Haskins won the first round, and advanced to the next competition while skipping the elimination bake.

The elimination mystery dessert was a frangipane plum tart, a buttery sable tart shell filled with a layer of frangipane.

The couple Bob Deluca and Vikki Deluca got eliminated from season 2, episode 4, Classically-Trained: Going Plum Crazy.

Crime Scene Kitchen Season 2 Episode 5 Recap

Crime Scene Kitchen season 2 episode 5 recap introduced four remaining self-taught bakers, among which only three teams who move forward will face the classically trained bakers.

TV personality Nene Leakes joined as a special guest judge in the first round safety bake.

The safety mystery bake was Quiche with Tomato and Mozzarella.

For the 3rd time, Steph and Cherry won the challenge and became the first team to be locked for the merger.

In the second round, the elimination dessert was a two-tiered lemon cake with vanilla buttercream and lemon filling.

Military Wives Kathleen Regelman and Hannah Reyes got eliminated from season 2, episode 4.

Crime Scene Kitchen Season 2 Episode 6 Recap

Crime Scene Kitchen season 2 episode 6 recap disclosed the safety bake winners, Sherry & Sally.

The safety dessert was Beef and Ale Pie with mushroom and beef stock.

Meanwhile, the 2nd round dessert was a two-tiered three-layer strawberry cut out cake with vanilla cream and strawberry jam between each layer.

Ricky & Dj got eliminated from Crime Scene Kitchen season 2, episode 6.

Crime Scene Kitchen Season 2 Episode 7 Recap

Crime Scene Kitchen season 2 episode 7 reveals the first team, Fadi & T, to advance into the quarterfinal.

The remaining team merged in the baking battle between Self-taught and classically trained bakers.

Special guest Award-winning singer and songwriter Martina McBride set the challenge this week.

Martina’s mystery dessert was Tarte Taitn with Apple.

Fadi & T won their first safety bake and advanced straight into the quarterfinal.

The elimination bake was Thirty crepe cake surrounded by a layer of chocolate ganache.

Twin sisters Sherry Mach and Sally Mach were eliminated from Crime Scene Kitchen Season 2 Episode 7, The Merge: A Total Crêpe Shoot.

Crime Scene Kitchen Season 2, Episode 8 Recap

Crime Scene Kitchen season 2, episode 8 recap shows the first team to advance into the semi-final are Yass & Amber. Steph & Cherry went home in the quarterfinal.

The remaining teams continue to fight for the semi-final spot, where television personality Richard Blais joined as a guest judge chef.

The mystery dessert was Caramel Pecan Sticky Buns.

Yass & Amber advanced to the semi-final as they won the safety bake challenge.

Elimination Bake was a 2-Tier 3-Layer Honey Anti-Gravity Cake.

Best Friends Steph Hsu and Sin Yi Cherry Lau got eliminated from Crime Scene Kitchen Season 2 Episode 8, The Quarterfinals: A Sticky Situation.

Some FAQs

Who went home on Crime Scene Kitchen?
