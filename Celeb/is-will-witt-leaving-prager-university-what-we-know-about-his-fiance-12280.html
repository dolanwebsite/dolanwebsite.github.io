Is Will Witt Leaving Prager University? What We Know About His Fiance

Prager U’s host, Will Witt, is rumored to be leaving Prager University and his fans are curious to know if the rumors have any truth in them and, if it is true, what he will be doing next.

Prager University is not an academic university with classes and certificates but an online political and current affairs information portal with videos and podcasts.

Many people have mixed opinions about the online portal and its content as it is popular among people for its misleading content downplaying the leftist and the issues brought to light by them.

One of the most popular hosts of PragerU’s content, Will Witt, is rumored to be leaving the portal, so let us learn more about the rumors and his personal life and future goals.

Is Will Witt Leaving Prager University?

Fans of Prager University are worried about their favorite host, Will Witt, leaving Prager University. However, there have been no talks of the host leaving the platform, and he has not addressed anything of the sort.

Will has been working with Prager University for more than four years now and has seen major success in the last few years in his podcasts and shows with the rising issues of abortion, COVID, and racism.

The podcaster has gained more than 700 million online views, and his audience seems to love him. After joining the portal, he has written a book and has even featured on different media outlets like Fox News, Blaze Media, and The Daily Wire as a cultural commentator.

With the love and support he is getting on his current work, there are fewer chances of the influencer leaving Prager University. Also, he has a lot of projects he is currently working on with the portal, like he recently worked on the Storytime with Will Witt.

Will Witt’s Fiance, Eva Vlaardingerbroek

Will Witt recently got engaged to his girlfriend, Eva Vlaardingerbroek, in March 2022 after dating for less than a year. The couple started dating in late 2021 after being introduced by Dennis Prager.

The PragerU star’s fiance is an opinion maker and a host who has made appearances on Tucker Carlson Tonight as well.

Eva is a former law student who studied at the Utrecht University and the Ludwig Maximilian University of Munich. She has even achieved her master’s degree in philosophy of law at Leiden University with honors in the essay “The contractualization of sex in the me-too era.”

Just like her fiance, Eva is also a right-wing activist and has published many opinion articles in newspapers as well. Both the couple have similar political opinions, including their opinions on vaccines.

Will is seen defending his fiance on Twitter, deleting her post about COVID vaccines, and bragging about being an anti-vaxer on Twitter.

Eva has worked for the party Forum for Democracy in the European parliament in Brussels but wanted to work as a researcher in Leiden. However, the young host left her position at the University of Leiden to pursue politics.

Where Is WIll Witt Going?

Although the chances of Will Witt leaving Prager University are really low with his ongoing fame with PragerU, Will, like many other influencers, may decide to go solo after a few years.

We have seen it with many influencers working with a bigger company going solo and developing their own brand after gaining a certain amount of fan following and fame. And Will has earned a big fan following by now, so it won’t be a surprise if the influencer decides to go solo and start his own show independent of PragerU.
