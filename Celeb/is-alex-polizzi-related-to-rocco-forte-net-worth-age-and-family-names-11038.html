Is Alex Polizzi Related To Rocco Forte?

Yes, Alex Polizzi is related to Rocco Forte, in fact, he is Alex’s uncle, an English hotelier, and the chairman of Rocco Forte Hotels.

Her maternal grandfather Lorde Forte, is an Italian-born Scottish hotelier founder of the leisure and hotels conglomerate which is now called the Forte Group.

Sir Rocco Forte is the son of Lorde Forte, and a brother of Alex’s mother, Olga Polizzi.

Her family is made of hoteliers from her grandparents to her brother-in-law, Oliver Peyton, Judge of “Great British Menu“.
