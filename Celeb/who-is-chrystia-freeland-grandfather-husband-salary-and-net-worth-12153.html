Who Is Chrystia Freeland Grandfather?

Chrystia Freeland’s maternal grandfather, Michael Chomiak, was a journalist before World War II.

He was the Nazi regime’s main editor of the Ukrainian antisemitic daily newspaper during the war.

In 2017, Russian-affiliated websites continued to promote his links to Nazism.

Later, Freeland and her spokesmen said it was all part of a Russian disinformation effort during her nomination as foreign affairs minister.

Likewise, her paternal grandfather Wilbur Freeland was a farmer and lawyer who participated in the Calgary Stampede every year.

Furthermore, Freeland’s grandmother Helen Caulfield was a Glasgow World war II bride.
