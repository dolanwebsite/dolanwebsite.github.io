Agape Mngomezulu Is A Canadian Actor

Agape Mngomezulu is 25 years old as he was born on January 17, 1997. Agape is an actor hailing from Toronto, Ontario.

Growing up in Ontario, Agape went to Delview Secondary School for his primary and secondary education. Delview Secondary is a school in Delta, Canada.

He graduated from the school in 2015 and started studying at the Vancouver Film School located in Vancouver, British Columbia. He graduated in 2016 and landed his first film role the same year.

While studying at Vancouver Film School, the actor also briefly worked as a Barista at a Starbucks in Vancouver. Since graduating from film school, Agape has been represented by Connekt Creative Talent Agency, also located in Vancouver.

Aside from acting, Agape is also interested in music and is a dedicated fan of the genres of Hip-Hop and R&B. Almost every caption on the posts on his Instagram account are lyrics from a Rap or R&B song. He is also a big fan of the rapper J Cole.
