Kathryn Dawn Lang OC AOE, known by her stage name k.d. lang, is a Canadian pop and country singer-songwriter and occasional actress.

Lang first earned international recognition in 1988 when she performed as “The Alberta Rose” at the closing ceremonies of the Winter Olympics.

On February 16, 2020, she was one of many musicians who donated their time to perform at Fire Fight Australia at ANZ Stadium in Sydney, Australia.
