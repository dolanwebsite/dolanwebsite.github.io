Jack Hughes’ Family

Hughes was born to his father, Jim Hughes, and his mother, Ellen Weinberg-Hughes. His mother was a former member of the United States national hockey team. His father was also a former hockey player and director of player development for the Toronto Maple Leafs.

He was brought up in Toronto, Ontario, with his older brother Quinn Hughes and younger brother Luke Hughes. Quinn was drafted by Vancouver Canucks in the 2018 NHL Entry Draft, and Luke was drafted by the Devils in the 2021 NHL Entry Draft.

His whole family is full of hockey players; he comes from a family of athletes as his uncles and cousins are also involved in ice hockey.
