Ladd McConkey Parents: Is American Football Player Related to Phil McConkey?

 Posted on December 12, 2023  |  2 minutes  |  347 words  |  Enda Jantzen

Footballer Ladd McConkey’s parents, Benji and Brittney McConkey were ecstatic when he committed to the University of Georgia.

Ladd McConkey is an American footballer and is a member of the University of Georgia Bulldogs football team.

The three-star athlete attended the North Murray High School before joining the University of Georgia. During his time at the high school, he was coached by Preston Poag.

North Murray coach, Poag, described him as the most dynamic player and the best leader he’s ever coached, according to The Daily Citizen.

He played as a quarterback, wide receiver, defensive back, kick returner, and even punter at North Murray.

Further, McConkey also received the Coach Mike Castronis Football Scholarship. He was named the J Reid Parker Director of Athletics Honor Roll for Fall in 2020.

Ladd McConkey Parents: Meet His Father& Mother

Ladd McConkey was born into an athletic family and supportive parents.

Ladd’s father, Benji McConkey, was a standout player at Dalton High School from 1991 to 1994. He was a quarterback of his football team in 1994.

His mother’s name is Brittney McConkey and is the younger brother of Hinton McConkey.

Hinton is also a football player and was a starting quarterback at North Murray. He initially committed to the Presbyterian College on a football scholarship.

Later, he transferred to the University of West Georgia.

Ladd McConkey Ethnicity And Nationality Explored

Ladd McConkey is of white ethnicity.

He was born and raised in Chatsworth, Georgia. He holds American nationality.

Is Ladd McConkey Related to Phil McConkey?

No, Ladd McConkey is not related to Phil McConkey.

Phil McConkey, aged 64, is a former American football wide receiver.  He has played for the New York Giants, Green Bay Packers, Phoenix Cardinals, and San Diego Chargers, as per his Wikipedia.

Ladd and Phil both share the same surname and are football players. It is probably why fans confused them to be relatives.

However, aside from their surnames, they do not share any relationship.
