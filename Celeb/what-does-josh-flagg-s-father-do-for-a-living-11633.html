What does Josh Flagg’s father do for a living?

 Posted on December 4, 2023  |  1 minutes  |  131 words  |  Enda Jantzen

Josh Flagg was born and raised in Los Angeles. He graduated from Beverly Hills High School in 2004. He attended the Brentwood School before that. He is one of the stars of Bravo’s Million Dollar Listing Los Angeles (2006). He is a real estate broker on the west side. His book came out in 2009.

Josh Flagg was born to parents Michael Flagg and Cindy Flagg. Josh comes from a family full of elites. His maternal grandfather, Herman Platt was a philanthropist, electronics industry executive and Jewish leader and his great grandfather Benjamin Platt owned the Platt Music Corporation.

What does Josh Flagg’s father do for a living?

The father of Josh Flagg, Michael Flagg is an actor and producer. One of his works that he is known for is the “Lady in the Park.”
