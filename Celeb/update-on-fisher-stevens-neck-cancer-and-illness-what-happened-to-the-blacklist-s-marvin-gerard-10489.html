Update On Fisher Stevens Neck Cancer

Some of the online users are worried about Fisher Stevens given his skinny neck, which people thought is because of some disease or cancer.

Most of his fans were concerned after they noticed that the director possesses a skinny neck that seems too small for his head.

But, when we searched it up on the internet, we found that he did have cancer in the past called Hodgkin’s disease.

It’s a type of cancer that develops in the lymph system. And what happens is that the white blood cells might have grown out of control, causing swollen lymph nodes and growths throughout the body as per the Mayo Clinic site.

According to Fisher Steven’s Wikipedia, he had lymphatic cancer, which implies that the actor was diagnosed in the past and might have already recovered from this disease.
