DWTS: Meet Brad Coleman Sister Brittany Coleman

Brittany Coleman is a talented dancer who appeared alongside reality television star Alex Vaz in the latest season of Dancing with the Stars New Zealand. She is the sister of another DWTS professional dancer Brad Coleman.

Brittany Coleman was eliminated on the latest episode of the show which aired on May 16, 2022. The couple was the fourth team to be eliminated in the ninth season of the New Zealand reality series.

However, Brad is still in the competition, having been safe in the May 16 episode, thus entering the top five of the series. He is partnered with author and mental health activist Jazz Thornton.

Brittany and Brad have another brother, Tyler Coleman, who is also a dancer. He is seen frequently in Brittany’s practice routines. The siblings have a beautiful bonding.

Meet Brittany Coleman Partner

Brittany Coleman is currently in a relationship with Marty Secker. The two seem to be immensely in love with each other. Though it is not clear whether the two are married or not, the two seem to be in a live-in relationship with each other.

Marty and Brittany seem to have been together since late 2020. Marty is first seen on Brittany’s Instagram in an October 2020 post on Instagram where the two could be seen being cozy in spring dresses.

The couple bought a house and lived together since mid-2021. Brittany posted on her Instagram on June 2021 that the two did a thing with the hashtag #firsthome, indicating they moved in together.

Brittany Coleman DWTSNZ Dancing Partners

Brittany Coleman was paired as the partner to the reality television star Alex Vaz in the latest installment of DWTSNZ aired in 2022. Despite the couple having perfect chemistry, they were eliminated fourth in the May 16, 2022 episode.

Likewise, in the last season, Brittany was paired alongside Clinton Randell, the radio presenter at The Edge radio network. The couple finished third behind the team of Laura Daniel and Shae Mountain and the eventual winners Manu Vatuvei and Loryn Reynolds.

Additionally, Brad was paired alongside author and mental health activist Jazz Thornton in the current series and with Olympic skier Anna Wilcox-Silfverberg in the last season. Similarly, in the seventh season of the show in 2018, Brad was paired with former Maori Party politician Marama Fox.
