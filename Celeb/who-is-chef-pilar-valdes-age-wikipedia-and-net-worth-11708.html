Who Is Chef Pilar Valdes? Bio

Pilar Valdes is an International Chef and the author of Rebel Homemaker: Food, Family, Life.

But, she wasn’t always as dedicated to cooking as she is at present. During the early 2000s, she was pursuing her career as a Deputy Director at the Global Action Project. In addition, she served there for 4-years and 11 months (from December 2003 to October 2008).

After that, she applied for the job as Program Associate at Funders Collaborative on Youth Organizing. Besides, she has had a keen interest in filmmaking and cinematography. She even pursued a bachelor’s degree in Cinematography from the University of the Phillippines.

Pilar has a total of two bachelors’ degrees, One in film making and another in Liberal Arts and Sciences.
