Gerald Celente Trend Forecaster

Gerald founded The Trends Research Institute (at first called the Socio-Economic Research Institute of America) in 1980. Prior to founding the Institute, he was a Government Affairs Specialist and an Executive Assistant to the Secretary of the New York State Senate.

Since 1993, Gerald’s forecasts have included predictions about terrorism, economic collapses, and war. He has also long-predicted global anti-Americanism, a failing economy, and immigration woes in the U.S. Gerald predicted turmoil which he described as “Obamageddon” in 2009. He was a popular guest on conservative cable TV shows such as Fox News Sunday and Glenn Beck’s television program.
