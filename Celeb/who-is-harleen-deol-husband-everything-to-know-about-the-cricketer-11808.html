Harleen Deol plays for Himachal Pradesh, an attacking right-handed batswoman who also bowls right-arm leg-spin on different occasions.

She made her Women’s One Day International (WODI) debut for India Women against England Women at Wankhede in Mumbai on February 22, 2019.

After Taniya Bhatia, she became the second lady cricketer from Chandigarh to play for India. On March 4, 2019, she made her Women’s Twenty20 International (WT20I) debut against England.

Harleen made her Women IPL T20 challenge debut for Trailblazers against Supernovas on May 6, 2019, and she and Smriti Mandhana scored 100 runs together.

She was picked to India’s squad for the 2020 ICC Women’s T20 World Cup in Australia in January 2020.

During the Twenty20 series against England in July 2021, Deol became viral after making a fantastic catch while avoiding the boundary rope. Sachin Tendulkar and Prime Minister Narendra Modi praised her for the yield.
