Career and Achievements

She started her career as a field reporter in Guam after completing her internship in Washington State. There she got to cover her own very first story.

She has earned numerous awards through her career. She received the “Best Spot News” award for uncovering the situations of unhygienic kitchens of a popular restaurant. Besides anchoring she also used to edit and produce news items. Through the hardships in Guam she gained a lot of work experience as a journalist.

After gaining two years of work experience from working in Guam, Lum moved back to her home in Hawaii in 2001 and joined KGMB 9 News. Her work in Hawaii was adored in the media industry as she earned 3 awards namely, “Best Feature Story”, “Best General News Story” and also as “Best Series Reporting”. She has also managed to win the celebrated award Emmy for the category “Overall News Excellence” in 2008.

The highlight of her career includes the coverage of stories like the arrival of President Barack Obama, 27th February tsunami and the October 2006 earthquake.
