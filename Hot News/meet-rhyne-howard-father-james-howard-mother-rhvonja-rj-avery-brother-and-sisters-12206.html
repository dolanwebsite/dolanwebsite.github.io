James Howard is the father of basketball player Rhyne Howard.

Rhyne is a professional basketball player. She currently plays for the WNBA. She played college basketball for the Kentucky Wildcats. She graduated from Bradley Central High School located in Cleveland, Tennessee in 2018.

Howard earned several accolades during her career. She was named 2018 Tennessee Gatorade Player of the Year and Tennessee Miss Basketball while her senior year of High school. Additionally, she participated in the USA team in 2018 and 2019, which led her to win the gold medal. She was also awarded MVP honors in 2018.

Following her freshman season at Kentucky, she was named USBWA National Freshman of the Year. In 2020 and 2021, she was named SEC Player of the Year.

Recently on April 11, 2022, she was drafted #1 overall by the WNBA’s Atlanta Dream.
