Kenzie Wheeler Biography – Kenzie Wheeler Wiki

Kenzie Wheeler is an American singer from Dover, Florida. He is a contestant in season 20 of NBC’s The Voice under the guidance/coaching of musician Kelly. Kenzie has been singing since he was young, but he did not take it seriously until high school when he performed karaoke at a local restaurant. His family and friends were impressed and encouraging, so he began going every Friday and eventually won a few local contests. He then made progress by playing gigs around town and even got chances to open for singers Charlie Daniels and Craig Campbell. He has a mullet that serves as his trademark. Outside of maintaining his mullet and music, he at times also works at a grocery store warehouse.

In November 2018, he released his full-length album, Dover, which is named after his hometown. He has also won a Tampa Teen Idol in 2016. The winning song was Chris Janson’s Buy Me A Boat. He has a YouTube channel where he uploaded videos of him singing, as well as some of his prior musical performances. He is also on Tiktok where he performs his music and has a pretty large following.
