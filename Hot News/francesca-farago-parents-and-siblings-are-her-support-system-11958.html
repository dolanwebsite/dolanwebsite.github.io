Francesca Farago Parents And Siblings Are Her Support System

 Posted on December 12, 2023  |  4 minutes  |  783 words  |  Enda Jantzen

Francesca Farago parents are Lucy Farago and Grant Webb. Farago grew up alongside two siblings Mason and Katie Webb.

Farago is a famous Canadian multi-tasker. She is a fashion model, social media influencer, television personality, businesswoman, and YouTube sensation.

The actress rose to prominence after participating in the popular Netflix reality television show, “Too Hot to Handle.” She has even worked in tv series like Love is Blind (2020) and Perfect Match (2023).

Mom And Dad

Francesca Farago father is Grant Webb and her mother is Lucy Farago. Farago has a mixed ethnic background of Italian and Canadian descent.

Farago is the first to try a modeling career, as her family is into business and literature. Let’s find out more about her father and mother.

Grant Webb

Francesca Farago father Grant Webb is co-owner of the restaurant “The Brew Table.” Before that, he co-owned the shop “The WORKS Gourmet Burger Bistro” for over eleven years.

Webb started his career as a general manager in May 1985 at O’Toole’s. He is a successful entrepreneur and has restaurants in cities like Ottawa and New Orleans. Besides, he is a fitness enthusiast and bike lover.

Webb met his love of life, Lucy, during his university years. The couple was college fellows. Webb enrolled at Carleton University in 1981, a year before his wife joined.

He graduated the same year as Lucy but with a Bachelor of Science in Geology. Before that, he went to Laurentian High School.

Lucy Farago

Francesca Farago mother Lucy Farago has been an author at Kensington Publishing since September 2014.

Lucy is an award-winning author best known for her romantic suspense novel, Thrilling Romance. Apart from writing, Lucy has been working as a fitness instructor at GoodLife Fitness, Ottawa. She joined the one in March 2004.

Moreover, she has been co-owner of MasonFrank Holdings, a firm that deals with bookkeeping, payroll, and budget management, for the last 32 years.

Regarding her educational background, Lucy holds a bachelor of arts degree from Carleton University. She graduated in 1986.

Siblings

Francesca Farago siblings are Mason Webb and Katie Webb. Both of them are younger than her.

They grew up in Vancouver and have already started their professional careers. Mason is in the kitchen industry, while Katie chose social media and modeling as her profession.

Mason Webb Is A Sous Chef

Francesca Farago brother Mason Webb is a sous chef at GB Catering. Mason joined the company in May 2018.

Earlier, he worked as a chef, garde manager, and supervisor for around four years in two restaurants.

Webb completed his health and physical education studies at Algonquin College of Applied Arts and Technology in 2019.

The siblings are close to each other and often gather together on their special days. On November 18, 2018, Farago and his little sister Katie went to Bevanda Ristorante and Pizzeria to meet Mason.

Sister Katie Webb

Katie Webb is the youngest in the family. Webb is the one who is closest to her elder sister. She even tattoed her name on her.

All three siblings went to the same university but chose different faculties. In the case of Webb, she completed her bachelor’s degree with international studies and honors in social justice in April 2021.

According to her LinkedIn profile, she has been working as the social media manager at BBTV since July 2022. She has more than four years of professional experience in social media management, content marketing, and branding.

Concerning her personal life, Katie has been in a relationship with Kai Mortan for several years. Morten appears on her Instagram account, with over 17.9K followers. She often shares her photoshoots from different cities and states.

Into Her Dating Life

Francesca Farago is now dating TikTok star Jesse Sullivan. The couple met on Zoom for a TikTok event in 2022.

At the live event, Farago interviewed three people, and Jesse was one of them. It was a love-at-first-sight moment for both, and the lovebirds immediately started dating.

Sullivan is a famous TikTok star with over 2.9 million followers. He makes random reels with his girl and kid. Likewise, he has 350K followers on Instagram.

The couple is seen flaunting their romantic relationship on their social media handles.

Before meeting Sullivan, Farago was in a relationship with “Too Hot to Handle” participant Harry Jowsey. The model met him in March 2019 and continued their relationship even after the show.

Yet, the lovebirds separated in June 2020 due to personal reasons and remained good friends. Earlier, Farago was rumored to be dating Love Is Blind co-star Damian Powers. They were spotted looking cozy even after the show.
