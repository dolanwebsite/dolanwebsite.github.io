The Artist Eve De Haan Age

The London Based Artist Eve De Haan is currently 29 years old.

She received a Joint Bachelor’s degree of Arts between UCL and King’s College London. She also has a BA degree and has specialized in the medieval history of the Middle East.

In 2015, Eve began her career as a Freelance Writer at IdeasTap. Furthermore, she has worked as a Creative Placement at The Corner London, a Marketing & Advertising Agency.

Eve’s artistic works are found provocative and challenging. She has also shown in the United States and at well-known European galleries such as the Saatchi Gallery in London and the Museum of Neon in Los Angeles.

Moreover, She has had billboards in London created for Nike & has also got featured in major publications.

Eve De Haan Ethnicity And Parents

Eve De Haan is a descendant of mixed ethnicity as she has the Mauritian and English flag on her Instagram bio.

Her biological parents are Roger De Haan and Marie-Lyvie M Goder. Her father, Roger, is an Internationally recognized businessperson who made many headlines in 2004 after selling his company for £1.3 billion.

Eve’s grandfather, Sidney De Haan, was even a more successful businessman in the family. He was the founder of Saga, an internationally known company providing a wide variety of services for people aged 50 and over.
