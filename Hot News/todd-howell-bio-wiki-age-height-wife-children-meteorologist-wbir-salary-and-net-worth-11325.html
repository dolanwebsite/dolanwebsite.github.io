Todd Howell Biography

Todd Howell is a well-known American Meteorologist who was born and brought-up in Gaffney, South Carolina. Currently, he works at WBIR-TV, an NBC-affiliated based in Knoxville, Tennessee as a Chief Meteorologist. Prior to joining the WBIR-TV weather team, he worked at WTVA-TV in Tupelo, Mississippi as a Weekend Weather Anchor.
