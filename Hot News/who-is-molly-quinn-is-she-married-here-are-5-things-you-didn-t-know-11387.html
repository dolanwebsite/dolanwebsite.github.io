Who is Molly Quinn?

She was born and named Molly Caitlyn Quinn on the 8th of October 1993 in Texarkana, Texas. There is a dearth of information on her educational qualifications and institutions attended, but she is obviously an educated lady.

Her acting career took-off at age 6, performing in The Nutcracker – the two-act ballet in her community. She was lucky to capture the attention of retired producer and director Martin Beck who proceeded to train the upcoming talent with weekly lessons. When she was 12, Molly Quinn auditioned at the Young Actors Studio, displaying her natural prowess for the scrutiny of veterans like director Linda Seto and some Osbrink Talent Agency reps; the young actress intensified her training after impressing them. Afterward, Osbrink Talent Agency signed her but Molly later moved to Management 360.

Her first acting role came on the set of Walk Hard: The Dewey Cox Story – an American Comic movie where she was offered a brief role in 2007. Following this, she had cameo appearances in Disney’s A Christmas Carol, My One and Only as well as The Sacrifice.

Her first major project came from Beacon Pictures and ABC Studios that signed her on to portray the character of Alexis Castle in Castle – an American crime-comic-drama TV series. Molly Quinn was quick to make good use of the platform in showcasing her talent which gave her a leeway into several roles on both the big screen and small screen. The year 2011 saw her on the set of Nickelodeon’s adaptation of the animated television series Winx Club, she acted as the character of Princess Bloom, while the show lasted till 2014. Meanwhile, Molly lent her voice to the same character in a couple of movie versions of the series.

From 2011 to 2017, Molly Quinn recorded appearances in productions like We’re the Millers, The First Time, Welcome to Happiness, Hansel & Gretel Get Baked, as well as the Last Rampage.

Is She Married?

Molly Quinn is yet to give up the single life in favor of matrimony; however, she is in a relationship with TV producer Elan Gale. They have been together since 2015 and it is beginning to look like the couple has plans for the future as their union has endured for four years and still counting. Evidence of their love can be found on Molly’s Instagram where she uploads beautiful photos with her man.

Molly Quinn was formally engaged to Chris Tavarez, they were together for a very long time but later broke up for reasons best known to them.

Height

The gorgeous actress is just 5 feet 5 inches tall which is approximately 1.67m. She sports light brown hair with eyes that are brown in color.

Trivia

It would appear that we are yet to see all that Molly Quinn has to offer because the veteran actress is also proficient in Karate. She is even a green belt holder in the sport.

Net Worth

Molly Quinn’s net worth has always come in variations, we have seen sources that pegged it at $1 million but, there are still a few who have taken it up to $3 million. However, her annual income is not known but she has appeared in some box office hit movies like We’re the Millers which grossed over $270,000,000 against a budget of $37,000,000 during the theatrical run.
