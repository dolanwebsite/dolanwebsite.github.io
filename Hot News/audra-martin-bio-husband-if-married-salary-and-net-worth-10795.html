Audra Martin – Bio

Working with Fox Sports as a sideline reporter and sportscaster who centers on everything about the Minnesota Twins baseball and Minnesota Wild hockey team, has made Audra Martin become a person of interest. She is well respected for her involvements with the news station and is highly praised for her eloquence on-air. Though she is doing perfectly at her job, Martin is not one who releases the details of her personal life at impulse. In fact, she is yet to disclose the exact details of her birthdate. However, records have it that she was born sometime in 1990 in Chicago, in the United States of America.

Similarly, Audra Martin is yet to disclose any information about her parents, but we already know that her little nephew, nicknamed Bug is one of her favorite persons. She has revealed this through her many picture uploads of him on her Instagram handle. She holds an American nationality and belongs to the white ethnical background.

The beautiful reporter had a fulfilling high school education. While in high school, she was a cheerleader and a two-sport athlete who excelled in softball and volleyball.

Later on, she furthered her education at the University Of Central Florida and graduated with a degree in Broadcast Journalism and Criminal Justice. While as a collegiate student, Audra balanced her social life and her studies perfectly, she participated in the school’s pageantry and of course, emerged as the crowned queen.

Career

Upon graduation, Audra got her first job at WAAY-TV, working as a sideline reporter for the Atlanta Thrashers of the National Hockey League. Having honed her broadcasting skills with them, she later joined the Atlanta Braves Organization. Subsequently, she worked with WKRN-TV as a Tennessee Titans sideline reporter and sports anchor.

Besides her job, the stunning reporter is empathetic towards cancer patients. Her many good deeds led her through the 2015 nominations for the Woman Of the Year by the Leukemia and Lymphoma Society. Even now, she has not put a halt in spreading cancer awareness throughout the globe. More so, while living at Florida, she supported the passing of a law that intends to put an end to online bullying; The Jeff’s Law.

Prior to the period Audra fell in love with broadcasting, she initially developed passion in other fields of endeavor. While growing up, Audra Martin was quite interested in music; she took classes as a violinist and was said to be quite skilled. She also had intentions of becoming a police officer.

Is She Married, Who Is Her Husband?

Audra’s Instagram pages have been filled with so many of her fans trying to get her attention towards the details of her personal life, however, the beautiful reporter has stayed schtum about this, but has gone ahead to give clues that she isn’t single.

She disappointed a lot of men who had an interest in her recently when she publicly stated on her Instagram handle that she has been in a relationship with her mystery man for a couple of years. In her post on the 14th day of October 2018, she further revealed that she met him a year after relocating to Minnesota. Sadly, Audra Martin has consciously hidden the identity of this mystery man who won her heart. Apparently, Audra is currently enjoying a flourishing relationship but is yet to be married.
