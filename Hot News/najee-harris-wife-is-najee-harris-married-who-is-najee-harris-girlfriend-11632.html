Najee Harris is an American football running back for the Pittsburgh Steelers of the National Football League.

Najee Harris was born on March 9, 1998, in Martinez, California, United States. He is one of five children of a single mother.

Najee Harris played his high school football career at Antioch High School in Antioch, California. He was considered the #1 recruit in the class of 2017, by Scout and Rivals. 247Sports rated him as the No. 2 overall recruit while ESPN ranked him the No. 11 overall recruit.

Najee Harris played college football at the University of Alabama. He was selected 24th overall in the 2021 NFL Draft by the Pittsburgh Steelers and was the first running back selected.

On May 25, 2021, Najee Harris signed his rookie contract with the Steelers, worth $13.1 million including a $6.9 million signing bonus over 4 years.
