Raymond “Ray-Ray” McCloud III is an American football wide receiver and return specialist for the San Francisco 49ers of the National Football League (NFL). He played college football at Clemson.

On August 21, 2020, McCloud signed with the Pittsburgh Steelers McCloud signed a one-year contract extension with the Steelers on March 11, 2021.

McCloud became the Steelers’ primary return specialist, returning 28 kickoffs for 646 yards and 29 punts with an average of 10.7 yards. He was named by Pro Football Focus on its All-Pro roster as a second-team return specialist.

In 2021, McCloud lead the NFL in total punt-return yards gained. On March 22, 2022, McCloud signed a two-year contract with the San Francisco 49ers.
