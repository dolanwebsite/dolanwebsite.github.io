Is Josh Weinstein Related To Harvey Weinstein?

No, Josh Weinstein and Harvey Weinstein are not related to each other. The only common thing between the two Americans is that they are related to the media.

Josh Weinstein is an American television writer and producer, most famously known for his work on the animated comedy series The Simpsons. He has also written episodes of other series such as Futurama, Gravity Falls, and Disenchantment.

Harvey Weinstein is an American former film producer and convicted sex offender. He is the co-founder of Miramax and produced several successful independent movies such as Sex, Lies, and Videotape, The Crying Game, Pulp Fiction, Heavenly Creatures, Flirting with Disaster, and Shakespeare in Love. After leaving Miramax, Harvey and his brother Bob founded a film studio, The Weinstein Company.

Though both Josh and Harvey are into cinema, the two Weinstein celebrities are not related by blood. However, both of them share the religion, they are Jewish.

Josh Weinstein Wife And Family Bio, Where Is He Now?

Josh Weinstein is married to Lisa Simmons, a West Coast editor of Cosmopolitan. The couple wed in a Jewish ceremony in 1995.

Lisa and Josh are parents of two children; a daughter named Molly and a son named Simon, both born in 1999.

Josh was born to Rosa and Harris Weinstein on May 5, 1966, in Washington, D.C. His father is a lawyer for Covington & Burling, and his mother is the director of the Himmelfarb Mobile University which provides education for the elderly. He has two siblings; a brother named Jacob and a sister named Teme.

He was raised in Washington where he attended St. Albans School where he met Bill Oakley in the eighth grade. In 1983, the two best friends created the school humor magazine The Alban Antic.

During his time at Stanford University, Josh served as editor-in-chief of the Stanford Chaparral. He is an honorary member of the Harvard Lampoon for his works on some of Lampoon’s parody publications with Oakley over the summers between course years.
