GAF Romantic Rewrite Filming Locations

Romantic Rewrite was filmed in Miami Florida, in 2022. The city is famous for its strong Cuban influence, pristine beaches, and great diving sites.

The movie’s plot location is in Florida, so viewers will get most of the astonishing Miami locations.

They will witness the joyous beach wedding with lots of fun, games, excitement, and beautiful decor.

Applauding the romantic movie, one Twitter user wrote, “Beautiful set and decor! I wish I could visit this wedding location in real life! Great casting in this one!” The user further commended the “witty and smart” scriptwriting.

Miami’s panoramic scenery, warm weather, and historic buildings make it a go-to destination for Hollywood when choosing film locations. From charming family titles to thrilling action films, it has been one of the top seeds.

Whether for a brief scene with an exemplary landmark or projects shot on-location to display the energy of the magic city, Miami has been featured in dozens of films and television shows.

Some famous titles filmed in the camera-ready Miami include HBO’s Ballers with Dwayne Johnson, USA Network’s Burn Notice, the autobiographical drama Moonlight, and the comedy Bad Boys Franchise.

Meet The Cast Of Romantic Rewrite

Romantic Rewrite cast includes Kristina Cole and Russell Quinn in the leading roles. Reed Favero and Allee Sutton Hethcoat appear in supporting roles.

Kristina Cole as Ivy Roberts

American actress Kristina portrays the lead character Ivy, a talented romance book editor, in the movie.

She is best known for playing Skyler Evans in the television series Storytellers, starring Joe Graceffa, Jessica Lu, and Jake Thomas. Cole has been in the industry since her debut in the 1993’s Proudheart.

Cole has since added 19 credits to her name as an actress. The Los Angeles native recent appearance include The Wedding Arrangement, Christmas in Pine Valley, and Miss Patti (2022).

Her other notable acting credits include playing Mrs. Hartley in Sam & Cat (2013), Juliet in The Real Housewives of Shakespeare (2016), Martha in Billions (2017), and Helen Litke in The Blacklist.

Russell Quinn as Reed Shepherd

Russell portrays another lead character, Reed, a mystery writer, in GAF’s Romantic Rewrite.

He made his debut with the short comedy, The Deported Trailor: The Spoof Trailor of the Departed, playing the role of El Colin in 2006. The following year, he appeared as Busboy in CSI: NY.

Quinn is notable for playing Miguel Guzman in nine episodes of Valley Peaks in 2009 and Elias in five episodes of Sunny Family Cult in 2018.

Besides being an actor, he is also a writer and producer. He is credited as co-writer and assistant producer for The Best Worst Christmas Ever, 6 Bullets to Hell, and This is Sinterklaas.

Reed Favero as Zach

Reed is an up-and-coming American actor and fashion model. He made his acting debut playing the role of Husband in the music video, Luis Fonsi, Juan Luis Guerra: Llegaste tu, in 2014.

Repped by Muse Models in New York, Fashion Models in Milan, and The Industry in Miami, Favero has modeled for many brands internationally. He has worked in campaigns for Ferragamo, Perry Ellis, and Express.

His acting credits include playing Javier in Wrecked (2016), Fashion Model in Ballers (2016), Russ Huard in Chateau Vato (2020), and Ian McCallister in Love Map (2021).

Suzanne Kovi as Harper Hugues

Suzanne has been in the industry for over two decades. She currently hosts her podcast, Flipping the Script with Suzanne.

She describes acting as one of her vehicles of self-expression. Though she began her journey studying psyche as a major and language as a minor, she soon realized acting was in her blood.

After graduating from Florida Atlantic University with a BFA, she immediately landed her first agent. Later in her career, she took a brief break to write her first book, Ignite Your Life.

Kovi is known for Passeur d’enfabts (1999), Madame le consul (2002), Daddy’s Home (2006), and Our Take on Love (2022).

Romantic Rewrite Plot Synopsis And Trailer

The GAF romantic movie aired on the channel on Saturday, February 4, 2023. The film was initially released in the United States on June 15, 2022.

The film introduces us to a romance book editor, Ivy, who is assigned to help a mystery writer, Reed, incorporate romance.

The writer agrees to be Ivy’s faux date for her friend’s wedding in return for her editing skills.

Trailer breakdown

The movie trailer offers a peek into the two leading characters’ tangled equations. The trailer was released in May 2022.

Initially, there appears to be a lot of tension and drama between the editor and the writer. However, the pair ultimately get along with each other during the process.

Based on the trailer, fans can look forward to a heartwarming romantic film featuring spectacular views and amazing cast members.
