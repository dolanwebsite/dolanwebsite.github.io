Dionne Baugh was 27 years old when she murdered her lover, Lance Herndon, after making love with him. Lance was the founder and CEO of Acess Inc., a computer consulting firm. He was a prominent part of the city’s young, wealthy and influential people.

Dionne Baugh’s Husband Shaun Nelson

Dionne Baugh’s husband is Shaun Nelson. He is a pilot for Air Jamaica. The couple got married and this gave Dionne an opportunity to relocate and move to Atlanta.

Dionne was born and raised in Jamaica. She grew up in a joint family and came to Atlanta, Georgia as some of her extended relatives immigrated there. The idea of the whole American dream called the young woman to marry Nelson.

Furthermore, in Atlanta, Baugh immediately started to work at the Metropolitan Atlanta Rapid Transit Authority.

As of now, Dionne and Nelson are divorced. Furthermore, Nelson has remarried and is living a happy life in Coral Springs, Florida.

Dionne Baugh’s Daughter

In 1992, Dionne gave birth to her daughter and the only child of the couple. They named her Amanda. However, their relationship started crumbling apart soon after.

Amanda Nelson is currently known to be studying medicine. Her life has taken a very different route than her entire family.

Where Is Lance Herndon Killer Now?

Lance Herndon was killed by Dionne Baugh, who was his lover. The 41-year-old self-made millionaire ended up losing his life at the hands of his 27-year-old lover, Dionne.

Lance had met Dionne in April 1996. However, during the time of his death, the couple was going through some disputes. Apparently, Dionne had seen Lance with another woman and made a scene outside his home by continuously banging on his door. He had to call the cops to escort her away.

As a result of the investigation, Dionne was charged guilty of murdered and was sentenced to 10 years in prison in 2001.

In 2011, she was released from prison and reports say that she stayed in Georgia since then. Some claim that she moved to reside around Florida and Kansas for short time.
