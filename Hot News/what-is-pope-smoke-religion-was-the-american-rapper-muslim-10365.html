American rapper Pop Smoke was Muslim, according to Reddit. His parents were Greg and Audrey Jackson. Let us learn more about the singer, his religion, and his background.

Pop Smoke was an American rapper who was born in Brooklyn, New York, on July 20, 1999. Pop was murdered while he was only 20 years old.

He died on February 19, 2020, while wearing a Rolex with diamonds. His Rolex, which they sold for $2000, was stolen by the murderers.

Pop’s parents called to him as Shar. Pop used to accompany his parents to a neighborhood church when he was a little child.

Pop Smoke Religion: Is He Muslim

Pop Smoke was from a Muslim background, according to Reddit. In his songs, he commonly utilized Muslim terms.

His funeral also included a Janaza in a Canarise Mosque, which is an Islamic funeral.

Bashar Barakah Jackson is his true name, which is also a Muslim name. However, he never claimed to be a Muslim.

Pop Smoke Parents Details

Greg and Audrey Jackson were Pop Smoke’s parents. His father is from Panama, while his mother is from Jamaica.

He was raised in Brooklyn by his parents and attended nine different schools. Obasi was his brother’s name.

Pop Smoke’s parents expressed their sadness over their son’s loss after he died. When Audrey and Greg heard that their son had been killed instead, they were crushed.

Pop Smoke Net Worth

At the time of his death, Pop Smoke’s net worth was believed to be $2 million.

He made money through his songs and live performances. In 2019, he was most recognized for his breakout hit, Welcome To The Party.

Similarly, his mixtapes Meet the Woo and Meet the Woo 2 in 2019 and 2020 made him a lot of money. Version 2 peaked at number 7 on the Billboard 200 list.

Smoke has also worked with Travis Scott, Chris Brown, Lil Tjay, Charlie Sloth, and Quavo, and some others.

Pop Smoke Death Cause

Pop Smoke, who was 20 years old at the time, died on February 19, 2020. He was assassinated in his own home.

Four hooded individuals broke into his Hollywood Hills home and shot him twice in the chest, according to reports.

A 15-year-old child was one among the four hooded guys who admitted to murdering Smoke.
