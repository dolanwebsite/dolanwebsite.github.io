Satish Ravilal Shah (Gujarati:??????) is an Indian film and television actor who is most known for his comedic roles in Hindi and Marathi films. He is probably best remembered for his performances in the enduringly famous 1980s comedy Yeh Jo Hai Zindagi, which was directed by Kundan Shah and Manjul Sinha and starred Manjul Sinha. He is well known for his performance as Municipal Commissioner D’Mello in the classic 1983 film Jaane Bhi Do Yaaro, which was directed by Kundan Shah and starred Anupam Kher. He is mainly known as a comedian, although he has also played a variety of parts during his career, which began with the 1978 films “Arvind Desai Ki Ajeeb Dastaan” and “Toofani Takkar” and continued into the 1990s and 2000s.
