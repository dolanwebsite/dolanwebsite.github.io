Hagar Ben Ari is best known for being a member of the “The Late Late Show With James Corden” house band.

She’s a fiercely talented bass player and the only female member of the band, playing to the left of bandleader Reggie Watts.

Hagar had the opportunity to tour Europe when she was 19 years old, and this is where she began her professional career as a traveling musician.

She toured with Sharon Jones and the Dap-Kings from 2010 to 2013, then supported singer Grace Potter while opening for the Rolling Stones and filling in at the Broadway show Kinky Boots.

Later, Ben Ari relocated to Los Angeles, and within a few days, she learned that Reggie Watts, the bandleader for James Cordon’s Late Late Show, is looking for a bass player. Soon after four days, she got the part.
