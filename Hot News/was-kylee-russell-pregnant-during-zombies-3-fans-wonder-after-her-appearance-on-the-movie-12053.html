Yes, Kyle Russell, the 25-year-old actress, was pregnant during Zombie 3.

Kylee Russell Pregnant During Zombies 3! Does She Have a Child?

Eliza- portrayed by Kylee Russell, is one of the prominent characters in the Zombies franchise; the fans were wondering why the character is away from Seabrook with minimal screen time.

The change in Eliza’s screentime compared with the first two installments of the film franchise was because actress Kylee Russell was pregnant around the time Zombies 3 was filmed.

Kylee’s recent interview with Girls United mentioned that she filmed the movie Zombie 3 shortly after having her daughter Greyson Blue Schmidt. She said filming the movie during pregnancy was a challenge and was a fantastic experience overall.

Kyle shared recently in an interview with Laughing Place about her filming experience of zombies 3; she said it was different as she is used to being with everyone, but this time, she had their recordings, and she was talking to the screen. She also added that it was fun as well as challenging to work in such an environment with her baby.

For Zombies 3, Russell brought her baby to the movie set. She also mentioned having her baby on stage made it easier not to worry about her baby Greyson.

Kylee Russell’s Husband Gavin Schmidt

If you have followed Kaylee’s Instagram account, you must have known that she has been in a relationship with a California-based artist named Gavin Schmidt for a long time. The couple knew each other long ago and started seeing each other.

The couple got engaged in 2021, after which she got pregnant with a child. The American actress gave birth to a beautiful baby girl on July 10. The young actress resides with her newborn child and husband in Los Angeles, California.
