    Steve Burguiere Radio Host

    Steve became an intern at the New Haven radio station KC101. He began in the promotions department, working his way up to the producer of the Glenn Beck Morning Show. He also did voice-over work and recorded commercials for the Metabolife company.

    Steve took over Glen Beck’s job after he left. He worked as a co-host of the morning show with Vinnie Penn. One year later, Steve moved to Tampa to produce the Glenn Beck Program, and host The Stu Show Saturdays on WFLA. Burguiere added bits to the Glenn Beck Program, posing as callers such as the Obama advisor “Honkey Whitesville”, a parody of real-life Obama advisor Jason Furman and “Wilfred from Sun City, Florida”. He also fills in as host for Beck as needed.

    Steve also co-hosted Pat & Stu with Pat Gray, a 2-hour program airing on TheBlaze weekdays at 12:00 Noon ET. He also the host of The Wonderful World of Stu, which aired Friday at 8:00 PM ET. In February 2020, Steve started hosting a comedy/news program, Stu Does America, on Blaze TV, Blaze Radio, and podcasts.
