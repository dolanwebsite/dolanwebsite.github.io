Scott Arceneaux Jr. is a popular American punk Rapper, DJ, Songwriter, and Producer who was inspired by T-Pain and Kanye West to produce music.

In Marrero, Louisiana, Scott Arceneaux Jr. was born on April 11, 1989, in the United States of America. He is the son of Scott Arceneaux Sr and Toni Arceneaux.

Scott Arceneaux Jr. attended a local high school before proceeding to the Delgado Community College. At school, he was hired to DJ parties. He bought his first laptop with money made from drugs. Rappers such as Gucci Mane, Lil Wayne, and Shawty Redd became his idols.
