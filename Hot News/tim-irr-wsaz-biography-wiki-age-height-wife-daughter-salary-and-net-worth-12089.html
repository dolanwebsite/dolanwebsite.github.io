Tim Irr Biography and Wiki

Tim Irr is an acclaimed American Journalist who was born and brought up in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania. Currently, he works at WSAZ Newschannel 3 in Huntington, West Virginia as a Reporter and Anchor.

Prior to joining WSAZ Newschannel 3 in 1995, he worked as a Media Relations Manager for Shadyside Hospital for three years. He as well served as the Assignment Editor at WTAE-TV from 1991 to 1992.

    Tim Irr WSAZ – TV

    Tim began his journalism career at WDTV-TV in Clarksburg, West Virginia where he worked as a Reporter. Currently, he works at WSAZ-TV, NewsChannel 3 as a Reporter and Anchor. He anchors the WSAZ’s 5:30 Edition, NewsChannel 3 at 6 O’Clock, the 10 O’Clock News on the Tri-State’s CW, and NewsChannel 3 Tonight.

    Prior to joining WSAZ Newschannel 3 in 1995, he worked as a Media Relations Manager for Shadyside Hospital for three years. He has served as the Assignment Editor at WTAE-TV from 1991 to 1992 and has also worked in management at stations in Charleston and Pittsburgh.
