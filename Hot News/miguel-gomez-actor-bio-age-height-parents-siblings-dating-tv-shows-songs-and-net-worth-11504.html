Miguel Gomez Biography

Miguel Gomez is a Colombian-American actor and rapper. He became popular to film audiences for his prominent role in the 2015 boxing drama Southpaw. He is also known for becoming the first artist officially signed to Jay-Z’s Roc La Familia Label. He starred as Ivan Ortiz on CBS crime drama series FBI: Most Wanted from 2021 to 2022.

Miguel Gomez Career

When Miguel began his career he first played a small role in the music video for Soul Survivor by rapper Young Jeezy in 2005. He made his television debut in the Miami episode of the FX comedy series Louie. He got his big break in 2014 when he landed the role of Augustin “Gus” Elizalde on the FX drama series The Strain. He first became known to film audiences for his prominent role in the 2015 boxing drama Southpaw.

Previously he rapped under the name Aztek Escobar and was the first artist signed by Jay Z’s Roc La Familia label before he began acting. His debut album was to be named Colombian Necktie but the label was dissolved before the album was released.
