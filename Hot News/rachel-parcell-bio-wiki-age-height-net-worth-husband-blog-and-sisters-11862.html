    Rachel Parcell Blog and Pink Peonies

    Rachel started Pink Peonies in 2010 as a way to document the first year of her marriage with Drew Rose. She gained an enormous audience who loved her daily outfits, home decor, style advice, and lifestyle posts. She named her blog Pink Peonies since pink is her favorite color while peonies are her favorite flowers.
