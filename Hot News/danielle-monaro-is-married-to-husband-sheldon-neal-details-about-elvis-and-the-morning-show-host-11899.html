Married Life Of Danielle Monaro With Her Husband Sheldon Neal

Danielle Monaro is married to her husband Sheldon Neal for 17 years now. The couple celebrates their wedding anniversary on 15th March every year.

Neal is one of the well-known real estate agents in Bergen Country. Neal proposed to Monaro Live on the radio during the ”Elvis Duran and the Morning Show.”

Neal and Monaro finally tied the knot in 2003 in a ceremony in the presence of her friends and family.

They have been enjoying more than a decade a long marriage without any separation of marriage or divorce whatsoever.
