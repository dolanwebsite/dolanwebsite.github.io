The Detty Sisters Ages and Family

 Posted on December 22, 2023  |  5 minutes  |  960 words  |  Lourie Helzer

The Detty Sisters ages range from 3 months old Mercy Detty to 14 years old Peyton Detty. Dettys welcomed the youngest member of their family on March 2023.

Widely known for their soulful voice, The Detty Sisters help the followers of the almighty connect with him. They have showcased their singing talent in numerous churches in different parts of America.

The Detty Sisters names are Peyton Detty, Caddie Detty, Lily Detty, and Sophia Detty. The newly added youngest sister of the sibling squad is named Mercy Walktin Detty.

They have released five albums out of which two of them include their parents. The albums are titled Freedom, Just As I Am, The Detty Sisters Family Album, Old Highway, and In The Meantime.

The young talented singers have also released 14 music videos of their well-known songs such as Thank God I Am Free, The Blood Covered It All, Oh How I Love Jesus, and No Doctor Like Jesus.

With their mesmerizing voice and charming personalities, the young singers have gained immense popularity at an early age. They have 165k subscribers on YouTube and 384k followers on Facebook.

How Old Are The Detty Sisters?

The Detty Sisters ages range from 3 months old to 14 years old. Peyton is the eldest while Sophia is the youngest at 7 years old until now.

Their baby sister Mercy is now the youngest. Below are the details on the age and birthday of each sister.

  • Peyton Detty

The oldest child of her parents graced this world in the year 2008. Currently, the young talent is 14 years old but she will turn 15 in the month of July.

  • Caidence Detty

The second oldest child in the household. She was born on June 21, 2010, and currently, she is 13 years old. Her loved ones call her Cadie in an enduring way.

  • Lily Detty

She is the middle child of the family. Lily is nine years old as she was born in the year 2014. The middle kid celebrates her birth anniversary on January 31.

  • Sophia Detty

The second youngest kid of the Dettys is seven years old. She came into this world in the year 2016 and her birthday falls on May 16. Sophia is nicknamed Curly by her loved ones.

  • Mercy Walktin Detty

Detty Sisters new baby Mercy Walktin Detty became the youngest member of the household on March 1, 2023. As of June, she is three months old.

Mercy’s birth video has been uploaded on the family’s official YouTube. The little munchkin’s mother has shared her pregnancy journey on other social media platforms as well.

The parents of the sibling squad announced the pregnancy news via a scavenger hunt.

The Detty siblings are homeschooled, they are taught by their parents. Peyton is in Grade 9, Cadie is in Grade 7, Lily is in Grade 3 and Sophie is in Grade 1.

The Detty Sisters Family Walks On The Path Of God

The Detty family is a gospel ministry from Frankfort, Ohio. The household is dedicated to spreading the gospel of Christ via, speaking, singing, and preaching.

The young singers’ family culture teaches them to follow and have faith in god. The two eldest children of the Dettys Peyton and Cadie accepted Jesus in their hearts when they were seven years old. The sibling squad wishes to sing for Jesus all their lives.

The talented girls began singing when they were babies at their home church. The first song Peyton and Cadie sang was Can’t Cross the Bloodline when they were four and two years old respectively. They were first introduced to the song by their mother.

The soulful voice of the young singers brought joy into the hearts of other people. In 2018, the almighty wanted the Dettys to share their faith all around the world with the gospel of Jesus.

With gods blessing the Detty Sisters are booking shows in advance all around the country. On May 2023, they were invited to minister at Barbour Avenue Church.

The young ones’ devotion to God can be displayed in their mesmerizing and captivating vocals. When asked, Lily wants to become a preacher when she grows up.

The Detty Sisters Parents

The Detty Sisters mom and dad are Amber Detty and Floyd Detty respectively. The pair are also part of the gospel ministry.

The couple first met each other when they were teenagers. The love birds exchanged their wedding vows on August 25, 2007. They have been married for 15 years.

Mother Amber Detty

The matriarch of the Detty household is also a gospel singer. She has collaborated with her daughters on the songs such as Down To The River To Pray, I Am, and Church (Take Me Back).

Sister Amber shares inspirational and motivational messages on her social accounts. In May 2023, the mother of five received her associates degree in Liberal Arts from Southern New Hampshire University.

She started college in 2014 when Lily was one month old but took a break for a few years due to her health issues. With the help of God’s blessing, she could complete her degree while taking on the role of a mother, and wife.

Amber celebrates her birthday on January 7.

Father Floyd Detty

Floyd is 34 years old and he celebrates his birthday on May 25. He is a proud girl dad who loves his daughters immensely and takes care of them with all his heart.

Floyd has appeared in several videos uploaded on the YouTube channel such as “We Fed Our Dad A Fried Sock,” and “The Detty Sisters Find Out They Have A New Baby Sister.”
