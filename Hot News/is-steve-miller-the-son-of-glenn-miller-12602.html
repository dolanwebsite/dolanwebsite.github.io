Glenn Miller was a trombonist, arranger, composer, and bandleader in the United States. He was the best-selling recording artist from 1939 through 1942, and he led one of the most well-known huge groups.

Glenn Miller died on the 15th of December, 1944.
Glenn Miller was supposed to fly from Bedford to Paris in the near future to finalize plans to relocate his entire band there. His UC-64 Norseman single-engined plane took off from RAF Twinwood Farm in Clapham, Bedfordshire, and vanished over the English Channel.

Helen Burger and Glenn Miller had two children, Steve Miller and Jonnie Miller, whom they had adopted.

Is Steve Miller the son of Glenn Miller?

Steve Miller, the son of Glenn Miller, is not the same as Steve Miller, the musician. Steven Haworth Miller is the leader of the Steve Miller Band, an American guitarist, vocalist, and composer. He started out in blues and blues-rock, but transitioned to a more pop-oriented style in the mid-1970s and early 1980s, releasing hit singles and albums.
