Kyah Simon Wikipedia Bio

Kyah Simon was born of Aboriginal-Australian ethnicity in Blacktown, New South Wales.

Simon began playing football with the Quakers Hill under-8s while growing up in Quakers Hill, New South Wales, near Sydney, Australia, in a family of rugby players.

She began her career with the Hills Brumbies before going on to Penrith Nepean United.

She fractured her leg just as she was about to receive a scholarship with the NSW Institute of Sport when she was fifteen, but it didn’t stop her from pursuing her passion.

At the age of 16, she made her national debut for Australia’s women’s national soccer team.

She went to Pacific Hills Christian School and Hills Sports High School for her education.
