Brandon Walker Biography and Wiki

Brandon Walker is an American sports gambling analyst working for Barstool Sports as the host for Walk the Line with Brandon Walker. He joined Barstool Sports on April 10, 2019. The polarizing analyst joined Barstool in the wake of a rivalry between Barstool and his former employer, MyBookie.

The rivalry began after MyBookie questioned the legitimacy of David Portnoy’s gambling habits. Portnoy, the founder of Barstool, hit back at MyBookie by leaking complaints from the site’s clients.
