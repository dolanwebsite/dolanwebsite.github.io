Is Emily Schuitema Leaving Wood TV?

People are wondering about Emily leaving Wood TV; she left the channel for more than six weeks. She, along with her colleague, announced that she would be leaving; she was an essential part of the team.

She told people that her part-time job as an environmental scientist has now become full-time, so that is the reason she is leaving the channel. In the last aired episode, she also said that she was very confused about what to let go of.

After that, her colleague threw a goodbye party for her, and photos are available on Instagram. She will be missed by her co-workers and fans who followed her weather update to run their daily lives.

Where Is The Meteorologist Today?

As of today, Emily must be on her work in Stantec, where she works as an environmental scientist. She started her job over there in 2021, and initially, she was part-time over there, but recently she joined as full-time.

She said that was the reason she left the channel. Her dedication to the world is really impressive; she used to help people by giving weather predictions, and now, she is working on the environment to protect it.
