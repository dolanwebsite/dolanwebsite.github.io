Belgium Footballer Michy Batshuayi Was Born To Congolese Parents Viviane Leya Iseka And Pino Batshuayi

 Posted on November 25, 2023  |  6 minutes  |  1107 words  |  Enda Jantzen

Belgian footballer Michy Batshuayi has Congolese origin from his parents, mother Viviane Leya Iseka, and father, Pino. The family moved to Belgium later.

This impressive striker has gained notability for scoring the winning goal for his national team against Canada at the ongoing 2022 FIFA World Cup. AS his Qatar ventures have made him known to much larger football fans, this 29-year-old is a man of his league.

Moreover, he was also a part of the Belgian Red Devils, which finished third at the 2018 FIFA World Cup. Hence, Michy has enjoyed success thoroughly in both his club and international career.

This goaler is affiliated with the Süper Lig club Fenerbahçe, based in Istanbul. Meanwhile, he has played with several notable teams, such as Standard Liège from 2011 to 2014, Marseille from 2014 to 2016, and Chelsea from 2016 to 2022.

Before his 2022 commitment with Fenerbahçe, he played on loan with Borussia Dortmund, Valencia, Crystal Palace, and Beşiktaş.

One of the amazing facts about his career includes his goal that won the Premier League title for Chelsea in his debut season in 2016.

With such amazing feats in his football career, Batshuayi’s parents’ support for him plays a big part; hence, his fans are eager to learn more about them.

Footballer Michy Batshuayi Parents Are From Congolese Origin

Michy Batshuayi hails from a multi-background with his Congolese origin yet Belgian nationality, thanks to his parents.

His parents had moved in from Congo before giving birth to this football powerhouse. Hence, Michy can play internationally for the Congolese and Belgian national teams.

However, this FIFA player cleared his intention in March 2015 that he would instead represent Belgium though his Congolese heritage is vital to him. Hence, he has been regularly playing for the Red Devils till today.

The Brussels native, Michy, and his parents travel around the world today to follow on with his football career.

Michy Batshuayi Father Pino Batshuayi Supported His Sports Career

Michy Batshuayi’s father, Pino had shown his love for sports, especially football, encouraging him to achieve a sports career. His parents understood his passion for the sport and enrolled him in youth clubs.

Hence without his father’s support, the striker’s current career prospects would not have been in the picture.

As a supportive father, Mr. Pino constantly travels, following his son in his matches and cheering for him on the ground. The senior Batshuayi refrains from social media and broad media questions since he only focuses on good communication with his children and family.

Michy Batshuayi Mother Viviane Leya Batshuayi Is A 

Mrs. Batshuayi is better known as Viviane Leya Isaka and is one of the famous football mothers in the current scenario.

Her openness to social media, especially Instagram, has gained an impressive number of followers since her posts give a sneak peek into her celebrity son’s family life.

Viviane accompanies her husband in Michy’s matches most of the time and is also the ice breaker, connecting to other players’ families. Her Instagram posts include her meeting with the parents of other footballers, including the father of Kylian Mbappé, Wilfried Mbappé, in September 2021.

Likewise, her social media posts indicate that this football mom is quite a brand shopper and has an impressive collection of designer bags and shoes. She is mainly seen flaunting big-named brands like Chanel, Hermes Birkin, and Louis Vuitton, among many others.

Interestingly, Viviane is confirmed to own more than two Hermes Birkin bags, considered some of the world’s most expensive and valuable designer products.

From moving from Congo to Belgian for better work opportunities, these football parents, Viviane and Pinto, have seen drastic changes in their lifestyles due to their multi-millionaire footballer son.

Michy Batshuayi Hails From A Family of Five With Two Siblings

Michy Batshuayi has supportive parents and a big family, including his two siblings. 

While he rarely talks about his whole family and his relatives in public, he only does so to respect their privacy. Meanwhile, he is more focused on his busy football days with the world cup than answering questions regarding his extended family.

This proud family man is happy to have his parents and siblings by his side at the moment.

Michy Is Younger Than His Sister Sarah Leya Batshuayi

Born as the eldest son, this 6 feet 1-inch football giant is the middle child of the Congolese-Belgian Batshuayi family. At 29, he is younger than his elder sister, Sarah Leya.

Their mother, Viviane, had publically announced Sarah’s presence in the family and her status as the eldest Batshuayi child. However, Sarah’s current career deets have remained away from the public though she frequently appears on her mother’s Instagram.

She is very close with everyone in her family, including her father, mother, and younger brothers. Her Instagram is @sarah_leya_batshuayi, though it is private as of now.

The eldest, Sarah Batshuayi, is careful about her Instagram posts and comfortable sharing with a limited number of her followers.

His Brother Aaron Leya Iseka Is The Youngest Sibling

The family’s youngest member, Aaron, is also a professional football player. He is currently playing for the Turkish club Adanaspor on loan from Barnsley.

He also plays as a striker and has also represented Belgian in the under-21 tournaments on international grounds from 2017 to 2019.

Following into his brother’s career, it is quite sure that the 25-year-old idolizes his elder brother. Likewise, the Batshuayi brothers can still play for the Congolese and Belgian national teams.

However, unlike his brother, Aaron suffered from Osgood–Schlatter disease when he was twelve. The condition forced him to take a break from football as it heavily affected his knee in adolescence.

Moreover, he goes by his mother’s last name, Iseka, contrary to his elder siblings, who go by their father’s surname.

This 6-foot-tall striker is a married man, having tied the knot with his wife, Shannon, in January 2020. This young couple shares two children from their lovely marriage. 

Likewise, Michy shares a daughter from his past relationship with Gitte Van Der Elst. However, The Sun in 2020 reported that he failed to pay her a whopping £100,000 in child support.

Ms. Elst sued him for failing to pay the child maintenance fund while missing two settlements and being late with several others.

Meanwhile, the Batshuayi family continues to grow with Vivian and Pino’s several grandchildren.
