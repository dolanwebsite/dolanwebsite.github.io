Penei Sewell is a 20-year-old NFL offensive tackle player who is currently dating Isabelle Ford. 

Penei Sewell is an American football offensive tackle player. He plays for the Detroit Lions of the National Football League (NFL).

Sewell was born on October 9, 2000, in Malaeimi, American Samoa. He is 20 years old. He is 6 feet 5 inches and his weight is 150 kilograms.

He went to the University of Oregon and played college football for Oregon Ducks. He was named as unanimous All-American and was awarded the outland and Morris award. Before, college, he went to Desert Hills High School.

Penei was also selected as co-receipts with Tua Tagovailoa for the 2019 Polynesian College Football Player of the Year award.
