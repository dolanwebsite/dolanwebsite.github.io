Where Is Daniel Petric Today?

At the age of 16, Daniel Petric killed his mother, Susan Petric, and severely injured his father as well.

Petric attacked his parents by saying he had a surprise for them. However, the surprise turned out to be devastating.

There was no death penalty given to Daniel because of his young age at the incident.

However, in January 2009, he was found guilty of murder and attempted murder with several pieces of evidence.

Daniel was sentenced to life in prison with a possibility of parole after 23 years.

He is currently incarcerated at the Grafton Correctional Institution in Lorain County, Ohio, and is eligible for parole in 2030.

Is He Released From Jail?

Daniel Petric had his trial on December 15, 2008, at the Lorian County Court for having his mother murdered and injuring his father.

He was sentenced to 23 years in jail on December 15, 2008, meaning he is still in jail and will not be released anytime soon.

Even though he is in jail, his family has been granted forgiveness. And his father keeps on visiting him from time to time.
