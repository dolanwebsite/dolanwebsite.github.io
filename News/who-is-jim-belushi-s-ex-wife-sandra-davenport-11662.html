Who Is Jim Belushi’s Ex-Wife Sandra Davenport?

 Posted on December 5, 2023  |  1 minutes  |  147 words  |  Lynna Burgamy

Sandra Davenport is the ex-wife of American actor, comedian and singer, Jim Belushi. Sandra Davenport and Jim Belushi were married for 8 years.

Jim Belushi and Sandra Davenport got married on May 17, 1980 and divorced in 1988.

Sandra Davenport and Jim Belushi gave birth to a son;  Robert James, who was born on October 23, 1980.

Sandra Davenport’s husband Jim Belushi is best known for the role of Jim on the sitcom According to Jim (2001–2009).

Jim Belushi’s other television roles include Saturday Night Live (1983–1985), Total Security (1997), and Twin Peaks (2017).

Jim Belushi has also appeared in films such as The Fury (1978), Thief (1981), The Man with One Red Shoe (1985), Little Shop of Horrors (1986), Real Men (1987), Red Heat (1988), Who’s Harry Crumb? (1989), The Palermo Connection (1990), Only the Lonely (1991), Traces of Red (1992), Destiny Turns on the Radio (1995), Angel’s Dance (1999), Return to Me (2000), The Wild (2006), The Ghost Writer (2010), Thunderstruck (2012), Home Sweet Hell (2015) and Wonder Wheel (2017).

Jim Belushi is the younger brother of late comic actor John Belushi and the father of actor Robert Belushi.
