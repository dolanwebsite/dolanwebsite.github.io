What Happened To Darius On Pitbulls and Parolees?

According to Tia Torres, Darius on Pitbulls and Parolees stopped showing up after he off work release from Dixon Correctional Institute near New Orleans.

Darius even mentioned he felt grateful to work with Tia when the episode aired. He felt blessed to deal with people like Tia, who was versed in animal care. He was motivated by Tia’s work with pit bulls.

Moreover, Darius mentioned he had supplementary ambition for what he loved to do. In September 2017, Darius led his first step toward becoming a vet tech. Dr. K teaught him to take an X-Ray. As a result, he experienced his first X-ray at that time. He was surprised at the moment.

However, he had worked under the direction of a doctor and found out the dog did not have a tumor and was not pregnant. He had even been introduced to Dr. Dozier. Even though Darius knew little about medical treatment, he was into learning about the process.

He has inspired by the people who had a drive like him and worked in the field they loved. Dr. K introduced every section of the veteran hospital and even spent time with Jessie. At last, Darius felt better chasing his dream.

Where Is Darius Now?

Viewers suspect that Darius have been released from prison after he served in a work release program and worked with Tia in her hugely popular Animal Planet show Pit Bulls & Parolees.

Darius’ Relationship With Tia Torres Explained

The founder of Villalobos Rescue Center, Tia Torres, was the one who allowed inmate Darius to be part of Pitbull and Porlees. He served on a work release program at Dixon Correctional Institute in Jackson, Louisiana. He had started working alongside Tia at the Villalobos Rescue Center.

Tia wished Darius replaced the center’s first-ever work-release care Country, Matt after he left for New York. During season 11, the show’s footage showed that she gave some points to Darius to train a pit bull.

As a result, Darius was finally made able to manage pit bull sit as Tia instructed. The inmate learned some valuable lessons in jail. The prison grew him to be a respectable young man.

Darius felt honored to receive the opportunity at Villalobos Rescue Center alongside Tia. He thought people needed someone who motivates them and pushes them for their ambition.

Tia felt the same about Darius and so did her fellow volunteer and previous inmate, Earl. Pit Bulls and Parolees returned with two episodes in 2017, where they featured inmate Darius in the second.
