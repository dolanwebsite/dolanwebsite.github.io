José Mara Ramos de León, best known by his stage name Joey de Leon, is a Filipino comedian, actor, and television host who was born on October 14, 1946. He is the presenter of Eat Bulaga!, a noontime variety programme. He is a part of the comedic trio Tito, Vic, and Joey, which has appeared in a number of films and television programmes. De Leon has written songs such as “Ipagpatawad Mo” (Forgive Me), “Awitin Mo, Isasayaw Ko” (Sing and I Will Dance), “Boyfriend kong Baduy” (My Geeky Boyfriend), and “Iskul Bukol” (Iskul Bukol) (School Lump).
