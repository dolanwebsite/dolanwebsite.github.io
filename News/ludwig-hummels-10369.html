Ludwig Hummels is the celebrity son of Mats Hummels, a German-born professional footballer. He plays as a central defender for the German Bundesliga club Borussia Dortmund and the German national team.

Dated for years before tying a wedding knot

Ludwig was born to his father Mats Hummels and his mother Cathy Hummels. His parents started dating in 2007 when his father was 19 and was a part of Bayern Munich Youth Academy.  

His mother was a cheerleader in her high school at the time. She moved in with her father and pursued her study at TU Dortmund. 

They dated for eight years before his father proposed to his mom during a Dubai vacation and married her on 15 June 2015. 

It was a private wedding at the Grand Hotel Bayerischer Hof in Munich. Cathy was named “Bundesliga WAG of the year 2013”.

His mother is a celebrity herself

Despite being known as footballer wife, Cathy is a celebrity. She has excelled in her career as a presenter and an influencer. 

She started to work as a columnist for Closer magazine in 2013. In July, the same year, she started working as a reporter for the ProSieben telecast, where she regularly visited Berlin’s Fashion Week. 

After her success and recognition, she later moved to Sky Sports as a presenter with her lifestyle program. 

Besides that, she is also an entrepreneur and advocate for a fit and healthy lifestyle. She launched a shoe brand, “Hummels.” In 2020, she hosted the shows in Thailand and Mallorca. 

Divorce rumors

There was a rumor of their split, which Cathy later clarified, admitting that they were not separated even if it was sometimes difficult. They always worked on themselves. 

During an interview with GALA, she confessed that the couple treats each other to everything and share happiness with their son. 

She also gave relationship advice telling people not to attach too much importance to the tingling in the stomach at the beginning of a relationship and be happy with what they have.

Ludwig is currently residing with his family in Munich. When his father moved from FC Bayern Munich to BVB Dortmund in the summer of 2019, he and his mother stayed back in Munich. 

His mother did not want him to move him away from the familiar surroundings since he was just a year old.  For his parents, his happiness comes first.

His face is always hidden 

His face is usually hidden in his parents’ social media posts. His face is either blurred, or an emoji is kept on his face. 

They keep on uploading pictures but do not reveal his face on their social media posts.

Grandson of retired football midfielder and manager

Ludwig is the grandson of retired football midfielder and manager Hermann Hummels. He also worked as a youth coordinator at Bayern Munich. 

Hermann works as a consultant for footballers in his own company, HMH-Sports management. Likewise, his grandmother Ulla Holthoff was a professional water polo player.

Net Worth 

Ludwig is just a kid and is currently relishing the lavish lifestyles with his beloved parents. His father has a glittering playing career with the best defending quality. 

He is regarded as one of the best defender in the world. He has earned 70 caps since 2010 to date. 

He has represented Germany at UEFA Euro 2012, FIFA World Cup 2014, UEFA Euro 2016, and FIFA World Cup 2018.
