Candace McCowan ABC News and Wikipedia

Candace’s journalism career started when she was still at University. She also worked with WVUA-7 TV, which was located on campus. She focused mainly on gathering information, editing, and shooting. She later went to work with WJCL/FOX 28 in Savannah, Georgia as her first job after college in 2007. Here she was a news reporter and fill-in anchor.

From Jan 2009 to Jan 2014, Candace moved to WREG-TV where she was a reporter for the morning show. This was followed by a stint as a news reporter/anchor with WFLA-TV in Florida. In December 2016, Candace moved to New York City to work with WABC 7 NY, Eyewitness News as a news reporter and is still currently working here.

Some of the top stories covered by her include Matt Lauer’s sexual misconduct and termination from NBC, Talking to President Donald Trump, and a story about a woman opening up about a terrifying attack on the Manhattan subway.
