Who is Don Vito? Meet LaTavia Roberson’s Husband

 Posted on December 7, 2023  |  1 minutes  |  195 words  |  Elina Uphoff

A native of the United States, LaTavia Roberson was born on November 1, 1981. As a founding member of Destiny’s Child, one of the best-selling girl groups in history and an R&B group, she first gained notoriety in the late 1990s. While a member, Roberson released two studio albums, moved over 25 million records, won two Grammy Awards, and won three Soul Train Music Awards.

Roberson temporarily founded the girl group Anjel after leaving the group, which also featured LeToya Luckett, a former member of Destiny’s Child. Those Jeans, How to Love, and Not My Family are just a few of the theater productions in which Roberson has acted as the lead. She joined artists like Angie Stone and Keke Wyatt as a prominent cast member of R&B Divas: Atlanta in 2014.

Who is Don Vito? Meet LaTavia Roberson’s Husband

LaTavia is a romantically involved with Don Vito, who she has been dating for a long time. The creator of the DVP Agency in Atlanta, Georgia, is her boyfriend. Also, he contributed as a composer to the TV movies Hip Hop Culture Odyssey: Know Your History and 2010 Soul Train Awards: Nomination Special (TV Special).
