Max Armstrong is a Chicago-based agricultural broadcaster who specialises in the Midwest. Armstrong co-hosts with Orion Samuelson the Saturday Morning Show and, formerly, the Noon Show on WGN radio in Chicago, where they have been since 2007. Armstrong is one of the most well-known and highly respected agricultural journalists in the United States, having written for many publications. Penton/Farm Progress Companies employs him as their director of broadcasting. 

He also serves as a co-host of the weekly television show This Week in AgriBusiness, which airs across the United States on weekends. He also contributes to a weekly hour-long agricultural programme on Chicago radio powerhouse WGN. Since Armstrong began working in the agricultural business almost 40 years ago, he has been seen and heard on television and radio by millions of farmers, ranchers, and consumers across the world. Having joined up with Orion Samuelson in August 2005 to co-host and co-found This Week in AgriBusiness, Armstrong has been a familiar face on television every week since then. Additionally, Armstrong has served as the director of broadcasting at Penton Farm Progress since July of 2009. He is the producer and presenter of the Farm Progress America and Max Armstrong’s Midwest Digest radio shows, which air daily. Armstrong also works as a freelance voice-over announcer via his business Super M, Inc., which he founded in 2003.
