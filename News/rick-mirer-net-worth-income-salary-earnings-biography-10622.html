Richard Franklin Mirer (born March 19, 1970) is a former NFL quarterback who played in the National Football League. Mirer was selected by the Seattle Seahawks as the second overall selection in the 1993 NFL Draft after a great college career at Notre Dame. In the off-season of 1997, Mirer was dealt to the Chicago Bears. Mirer last played in the NFL in 2004 as a backup quarterback for the Detroit Lions. Mirer also played for the New York Jets, San Francisco 49ers, and Oakland Raiders throughout his career, but he was never able to justify his high draught selection. He currently works as a football coach for the Torrey Pines Pop Warner programme. Mirer is also a co-owner of the Mirror Wine Company in Napa County, California. His Mirer Family Foundation, which he established in 1996, receives a portion of the profits from wine sales.
