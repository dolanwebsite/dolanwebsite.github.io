Leigh Waldman Biography

Leigh Waldman is a renowned American Journalist and Television Personality who was born in Los Angeles and brought-up in Dallas, Fort Worth, Texas. Currently, she works at WOWT channel 6 in Omaha, Nebraska as a Weekend Morning Anchor and a Weekday Reporter.

Prior to joining the WOWT news team, she worked at Newscenter 25 as a News Anchor, Producer and On-Air Talent for about 2 years. She served as a News Anchor for the TTU sports entertainment show Raider Report, at CNN as an Atlanta Bureau Intern, and as a News Intern at Ramar Communications KJTV FOX 34 for 5 months.
