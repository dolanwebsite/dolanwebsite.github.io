Channing Tatum has one sister named Paige Tatum. She is the younger sister of Channing and has largely stayed out of the public eye.

Channing has also mentioned a half-brother named Christopher Anderson in interviews, but little is known about him.

Channing has spoken publicly about his close relationship with his sister and the importance of family in his life.

Paige Tatum and Christopher Anderson’s siblings, Channing Tatum is an American actor, producer, and dancer. He was born on April 26, 1980, in Cullman, Alabama, USA. Tatum began his career as a model before transitioning to acting, and his breakthrough role was in the 2006 dance film “Step Up”.
