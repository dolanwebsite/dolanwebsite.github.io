Billy Palmer: Who Is Jesse Palmer’s Brother?

 Posted on December 27, 2023  |  1 minutes  |  188 words  |  Elina Uphoff

Billy Palmer is a former football tight end for Notre Dame, and the younger brother of Jesse Palmer.

Billy bears the nickname “Gonzo” and does not seem to be interested in TV work like his brother.

Who Is Jesse Palmer?

Jesse Palmer is a former football commentator and analyst for ESPN and the SEC Network.

Palmer has also been associated with the shows “Good Morning America” and “Live with Kelly and Michael”, and the “Holiday Baking Championship” on the Food Network.

Palmer made his TV debut in 2004 when he appeared on the fifth season of the reality show “The Bachelor”, which he now hosts.

Before launching his TV career, Palmer was a professional footballer who played for the New York Giants as a quarterback.

He played college football at the University of Florida, where he was the starting quarterback for the Gators from 1999 to 2000.

He was drafted by the Giants in the fourth round of the 2001 NFL Draft.

Palmer’s NFL career saw him play for five seasons for the Giants, the San Francisco 49ers and the Montreal Alouettes of the Canadian Football League (CFL).
