Dan Graziano Biography

Dan Graziano is an American sports journalist who is currently working as an NFL Insider for ESPN. He became part of ESPN in 2011 as an NFC East blogger. As a blogger, he reports on the Dallas Cowboys, Philadelphia Eagles, Washington Football Team, and New York Giants. He also provides live reports on Sunday NFL Countdown as well as Fantasy Football Now.
