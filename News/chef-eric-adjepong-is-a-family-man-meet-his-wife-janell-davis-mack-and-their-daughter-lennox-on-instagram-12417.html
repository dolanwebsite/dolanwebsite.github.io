Chef Eric Adjepong, a much-loved participant in seasons 16 and 17, would be familiar to Top Chef fans. He was the first participant on the show to fully highlight West African foods and flavors as a Ghanaian-American.

Eric Adjepong is a Washington, D.C.-based culinary (personal) chef, caterer, and public health nutrition professional. He was a finalist on Bravo’s Top Chef season 16 and a contestant on Top Chef All-Stars season 17, where he received regular praise from the judges and gained popularity among fans.

He went on to work at various Michelin-starred restaurants, notably Rouge Tomate, after graduating from Johnson & Wales. His West African cuisine serves as a source of inspiration for his cooking.

Who Is Eric Adjepong Wife Janell Davis-Mack?

Janell Davis-Mack, an interior designer, is his wife. The pair met through a simple Facebook conversation. 

Janell works as a human resources specialist and a decor consultant. She is the principal human resources specialist at the Food and Drug Administration and a graduate of George Washington University.

The couple got engaged in September 2016 after dating for several years. According to the essence online, Eric and Janell exchanged wedding wows on May 21, 2017. Similarly, they married in the Downtown Club in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, in front of their closest relatives and friends.

He operates ‘Pinch & Plate,’ a personalized, full-service dinner party service held at homes or venues with his wife. “All that is required is a dining table with chairs to accommodate the party size, access to a fully functional kitchen, and Pinch & Plate handles the rest,” according to the company’s website.

Furthermore, they have a beautiful daughter together, named Lennox.

Details About Eric Adjepong Family Explored

Chef Adjepong may have a wonderful family, but he has yet to publicly reveal every one of them to the public. His father’s name is Benjamin Adjepong.

Adjepong grew up in the Bronx in a traditional Ghanian family. He was the first of his family to be born in the United States. His parents arrived from Ghana’s Kumasi.

As a child, the chef was mesmerized by the sight of his mother and other relatives preparing meals in the kitchen. Adjepong claims that his mother “was always trying to develop new recipes.” When it came to producing traditional Ghanaian cuisine, “she was all over it,” ensuring they became a household staple.

Adjepong is one of the few Ghanaian chefs he knows, and he considers himself fortunate to have received “a lot of support” from his family to pursue a career in the culinary arts.

How Much Is Eric Adjepong Net Worth?

Eric Adjepong’s net worth is reported to be between $3 million and $5 million. The restaurant and catering industries are his principal sources of revenue.

He is a celebrity chef and caterer whose name appears on the Top Chef: Kentucky Wikipedia page. He may be lavishly compensated and receive a solid check as a well-known culinary expert and unscripted television star. In addition, he is additionally an investor in City-State Brewery.

He has massive followers on Instagram (@chefericadjepong) with a staggering 70k people and 430 posts on the handle. He often posts about his new dishes, daughter, and personal life.
