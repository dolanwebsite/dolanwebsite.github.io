Cristian Alberto Cueva Bravo or simply Cristian Cueva is a professional footballer who plays as an attacking midfielder for the Peru national team and Brazilian professional football club São Paulo.

In addition to playing for several clubs in Peru. He also played for Mexican football club Toluca in 2015 and represented Peru in the youth team.

After making his debut in Peru in 2016, he played in the Copa America in 2015 and the Copa America Centenario in 2016.
