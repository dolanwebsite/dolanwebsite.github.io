Biography

Sarah Tiana was born as Sarah Tiana Haynes in Hayward, California on the 17th of February 1978. She was brought up together with her sister, Joanna Tiana in Calhoun, Georgia. She is an avid animal lover who owns a Pitbull named Maverick. For her academics, Sarah Tiana attended Gordon Central High School located in Calhoun, Georgia. Soon after her graduation from high school, she furthered her studies at the University of Georgia.

Due to her commitments at Six Flags, a recreational park in Cobb County which is far from the University of Georgia campus in Athens, the smart-witted TV host transferred to Georgia State University in 1997. She then graduated with a Bachelor of Arts Degree in Theatre, majoring in Film. While still at the university, Tiana went to study Commedia Dell’arte at Sorbonne, France during the summer of 1999.

Few years after her graduation, she relocated to Los Angeles and nabbed her first job as a waitress in 2003. Also in the same year, Sarah Tiana kicked off her acting career by clinching her breakout role as Carmen in the 2003 American comedy television series, Reno 911. Her outstanding performance in the aforementioned TV series fetched the beautiful actress more film and television offers as she was chosen to play the role of Sandy Hoak, in the crime thriller movie, White Trash Noir.

In addition, the apt comedian has gone on to appear in quite a number of projects which includes, Comics on Duty: We Love You Mrs Bevins, Comedy Central Roast of Rob Lowe and Brett Butler presents the Southern Belles of Comedy. Moving on from her acting career, the quick-witted comedian co-founded a sketch comedy company called ‘The Straight Jacket Society’, with the sole aim of aiding comedians, new actors and entertainers to find their feet in Hollywood without losing the fun in the process.

For her work as a comedian, Sarah Tiana toured together with Danny Bevins, Dave Mishevitz and John Bizarre to inspire the troops in the United States military bases around the world. In addition, she has been a frequent guest in several popular television shows such as Brad Paisley’s Comedy Rodeo, Chelsea lately, @ Midnight on Comedy Central, and Crashing.

Beyond her works as a comedian and actress, the skilled TV host also serves as a writer for Jeff Ross and The Burn and the Josh Wolf Show on CMT Network. Apart from being the host of CMA’s Red Carpet, Sarah Tiana further works as a co-host of two podcasts; Southern Comedy Podcast, Fried with Jon Reep and Riggle’s Picks with The Daily Show alum Rob Riggle, as previously mentioned.
