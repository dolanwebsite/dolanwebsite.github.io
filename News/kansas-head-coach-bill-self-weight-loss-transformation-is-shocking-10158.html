Bill Self had a weight loss transformation during the off-season. The head coach went under abdominal surgery in the spring of 2004.

Bill Self Weight Loss Transformation: Before And After

The Kansas coach, Bill Self, is known for his weight loss as much as he is notable for his coaching on the court. His transformation to a healthy lifestyle shocked many.

The most intriguing part of his weight loss transformation is that while people have been concerned about his belly fat throughout the years, it is quite prominent that the coach loses off-season.

KC Confidential, in 2017 reported on the issue while taking notes of several basketball fans who had noticed the coach’s visible belly fat. A KU fan revealed that Self weighed about 250 to 300 pounds in 2016, which could be a result of his unhealthy lifestyle and consumption of beer. 

Moreover, it could have been his diet at Salty Iguana, where he is a part owner and enjoys delicious meals most of the time.

Meanwhile, the report showed a possibility of Cushing’s syndrome, a condition in which excessive exposure to the stress hormone cortisol causes weight gain and other abnormalities.

However, this NCAA coach grabbed eyeballs in the 2018 off-season when he appeared healthier than ever. His gut had shrunk back in, and he looked more beneficial than before.

He may have gotten off his hectic lifestyle, but gaining such easy results was not easy. Hence, his quick weight gain and loss have captured the attention of many on social media who are now eager to know the truth behind the transformation.

While many suggest that it could have been surgery, others retaliate by saying the coach could pull himself to the gym and eat a healthy diet each day.

Meanwhile, LJ World confirms that Self had abdominal surgery in the spring of 2004. His scar remains intact, at least during the 2009 reporting of it. However, the precise particulars of the surgery’s purpose remain confined to the award-winning coach.

Kansas Head Coach Bill Self Diet Workout And Healthy Lifestyle

Bill Self may be quiet about his diet or workout routine, but he follows a healthy lifestyle as a basketball team coach.

However, he is more focused on the Jayhawks as he constantly changes the players’ nutrition measurements. The last initiation by the coach was made public in 2016 when he teamed up with the team’s nutritionist, pre Training & Conditioning.

His Twitter posts show that the coach is a “foodie” since he enjoys divulging delicious food with a companion. Moreover, Self has been far from sharing his habits of visiting Salty Iguanas as of 2022.

This basketball expert is more adamant about speaking about the Jayhawks and its progress rather than his personal matters.
