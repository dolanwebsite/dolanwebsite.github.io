Santos “Sandy” Alomar Velázquez, Jr. (Spanish: [alo?mar], /?l?m?r/; born June 18, 1966) is a professional baseball catcher, manager, and coach. Between 1988 and 2007, he was a catcher in Major League Baseball with the San Diego Padres, Cleveland Indians, Chicago White Sox, Colorado Rockies, Texas Rangers, Los Angeles Dodgers, and New York Mets. Alomar has been named to the All-Star team six times. He is the younger brother of Hall of Fame second baseman Roberto Alomar and the son of former big leaguer Sandy Alomar, Sr.
