Darryl Mccauley Death: Truth Or Hoax?

No, Darryl Mccauley is not dead, as no official announcement has been made as of yet. It’s just hearsay at this point.

He was born in 1965 and is presently 56 years old in 2021.

Darryl Mccauley: Dane Cook’s Brother Story

Dane Cook entrusted Darryl with his money management throughout the height of his success until 2008, when it was found that Darryl and his wife had embezzled millions of dollars from Dane.

Following that, the Boston Globe reported in 2010 that Darryl pled guilty to embezzling millions from his renowned half-bro while acting as his business manager. He was sentenced to five to six years in a Massachusetts state prison for this offense.

According to the Attorney General in a statement released following the trial. Darryl easily moved money from Dane Cook’s bank accounts to his own or just wrote huge cheques to himself,

Darryl poured millions of dollars into his own accounts during that period. Darryl was then forced to reimburse Dane $12 million by the court.

In the meantime, Darryl was sentenced to five to six years in jail. His wife received a sentence of a few years in jail as well.

Despite the fact that his brother cheated him out of millions of dollars and despite the fact that he is still doing well financially. According to Celebrity Net Worth, he still has $35 million in the bank.
