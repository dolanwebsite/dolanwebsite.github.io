Boxing Trainer Joe Goossen Wikipedia

Joe Goossen is the son of Elliott “Al” Goossen and Anna Mae Goossen. His parents have ten kids, including him and he was 7th. He grew up with 7 brothers and 2 sisters. All of them were very close to him.

He grew up in San Fernando Valley and now also he lives there with his family. He has a wife Julia L Neale with whom he married on September 25, 1976. The couple has four children together.

His family is very proud of his achievements and has been very supportive of him on his path to success. It looks like Joe has a long career in boxing because he is making quite a progress to date.

Joe Goossen Net Worth 2022 Explored – How Rich Is He?

Joe has been estimated to be worth around $11 million. He garnered his wealth from his successful career as a boxing trainer to some of the best players in the present scenario of the boxing world.

He is also paid well for his work on FOX, where he acts as their sports analyst. It is the place where people can see him giving depth to the game in a manner that common people can understand.

It looks like his net worth will increase in the future because he seems to be taking more positions as a trainer and his coordination with the current star Ryan can be seen as a sign of it.

Who Has Joe Goossen Coached?

Over the decades of his career in boxing coaching, Joe has trained stars like the Ruelas – brothers Gabriel and Rafael, Shane Mosley, Amir Khan, Michael Nunn, Riddick Bowe, Lionel Butler, and Diego Corrales, to take some big names.

Many might not know that he is the brother of Dan Goossen. Dan was a boxing promoter and agent, who promoted big names like Mike Tyson, David Tua, James Toney, Paul Williams, and Floyd Mayweather.

Ryan seems to be excited to get Joe as his trainer and has some high hopes for him.
