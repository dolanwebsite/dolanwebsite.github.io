Chris Warren Jr. Biography

Chris Warren Jr. is an American Actor who was born and brought up in Indianapolis, Indiana. He is widely known for his role in the musical televisio films ‘High School Musical franchise’ as Zeke Baylor, the ABC mystery drama television series ‘Grand Hotel’ as Jason Parker and in the Disney channel ‘Good Luck Charlie’ as Justin.

Chris is also known for his appearances on notable films and series including ‘Alvin and the Chipmunks: The Squeakquel’ as Xander, ‘The Bold, ‘Men of Honor’ as Young Carl Brashear, and the Beautiful’ as Jimmy Ramirez and in ‘The Hard Times of RJ Berger’ as Patterson.
