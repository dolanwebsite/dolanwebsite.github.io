Shannon Sharpe’s Wife Died Or Left Him?

In 2016, Shannon Sharpe was engaged to his ex-wife, Katy Kellner, an American fitness trainer. No such reports claiming her death have been released.

According to Focus, the couple’s five-year romance came to an end after Kellner had a kid with another guy.

Shannon and Katy encountered in 2013 at a Thousand Oaks gym and began dating right away, as per Nailbuzz.

The couple got engaged in 2016. Katy, on the other hand, became pregnant in early 2018 with Marlon Byrd, her business partner.

Details On Shannon Sharpe’s Daughter Kayla And Sons

Shannon has three children: Kayla and Kaley, both daughters, and Kiari, a son. All of his children are the result of his three failed romances.

Kayla, his eldest daughter, and Kiari, his only son, are both 29 years old. Kiari pursued biology and business management at Georgia Southern, while Kayla attended pre-law at Georgia Southern, as per the Denver Post.

According to the same report, Kaley went to Florida State with the intention of being a medical examiner.

The kids, on the other hand, reside with their respective mothers in Georgia and Florida, and Shannon pays them frequent visits.

Does Shannon Sharpe Have A Girlfriend In 2022?

Shannon Sharpe does not have a girlfriend in 2022. The former Baltimore Ravens tight end has kept his personal life hidden from the public eye throughout the years.

However, back then, Sharpe was in the news as his ex-girlfriend Michelle Bundy filed a sexual assault lawsuit. The case was eventually closed. Bundy was allegedly accused of inventing her claims against the former player in order to attract media attention.

Furthermore, he made news again in 2017 when he was associated with American fashion model Nicole Murphy after a decade of seclusion.
