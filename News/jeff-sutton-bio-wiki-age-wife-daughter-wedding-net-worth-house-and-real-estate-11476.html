Jeff  Sutton Biography

Jeff Sutton is an American founder and president of Wharton Properties and one of the most prolific retail landlords in New York City. He and his firm own vast amounts of retail space in the five boroughs, including marquee addresses such as 1551 and 1515 Broadway, 141 Fifth Avenue, 717 and 720 Fifth Avenue as well as 379 West Broadway.

All totaled, Sutton controls over 120 properties in the city. Also, he is a regular partner on acquisitions and sales with SL Green Realty, the city’s largest commercial landlord.

Sutton began by brokering store sites for Payless Shoes in the outer boroughs. He gradually parlayed that into a major fortune. His clients as well as tenants have included brand names such as American Eagle, Nike, and Prada. He also has a summer home in Deal, New Jersey.
