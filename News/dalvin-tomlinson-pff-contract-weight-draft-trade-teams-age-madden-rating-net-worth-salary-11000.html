Dalvin Tomlinson Contract

Dalvin Tomlinson signed a two-year, $21 million contract with the Minnesota Vikings, which included a $12,500,000 signing bonus, $20,800,000 in guaranteed and a $10,500,000 average annual salary.

Dalvin Tomlinson Draft

Tomlinson was drafted by the New York Giants in the second round of the 2017 NFL Draft, and played for the team from 2017 to 2020 and later signed with the Minnesota Vikings.
