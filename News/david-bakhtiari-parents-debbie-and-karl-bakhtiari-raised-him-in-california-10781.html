David Bakhtiari was raised by his parents, Debbie Bakhtiari and Karl Bakhtiari, in California. The Offensive Tackle grew up as one of four kids.

He is an American football player who plays for the Green Bay Packers of the National Football League (NFL).

Bakhtiari’s splendid performances in football matches during school paved the way for him to excel in this sector as an emerging player.

Eventually, during college, he developed into a better player and was drafted by his immediate team, the Green Bay Packers, as an offensive tackle during the 2013 NFL Draft.

David Bakhtiari Parents Debbie And Karl Bakhtiari

David Bakhtiari grew up with parents Karl Bakhtiari and Debbie Bakhtiari in San Mateo, California. However, he currently resides in Burlingame, California.

Debbie could be spotted on Instagram via David’s photo tags with the name Devilina Bakhtiari which is maintained private and has around 200 followers.

Karl and Debbie have been married for nearly three decades. And they have raised four talented children growing up to be the best versions of themselves in the professional fields they have dreamt of.

His Father Karl Bakhtiari Has A Successful Business

Talking about David’s father, Karl, has a real estate business in California and is of Iranian and Icelandic descent.

Karl Bakhtiari founded Stonesfair Financial Corporation (SFC), a real estate investment company, in the year 1992. And he serves there as the president and CEO of SFC.

Since 1993, he has grown the company to over $500 million in assets under management. The company owns and operates over 2,000 multifamily properties, retail centers, and office buildings in California, Arizona, Montana, and Hawaii.

The SFC firm, which Bakhtiari and Maryann Fair lead, has been active in the marketplace since 1993. Thus, it has won many highly competitive negotiations.

They believe that their success is due to their skills and experience and their hands-on approach to acquisitions and management.

Bakhtiari is also the 2003 recipient of the College of Business Distinguished Alumni Award. Eventually, he served on the University’s Board of Governors and Business Advisory Board.

Likewise, Karl is a co-owner of the Sundance Ranch winery, a 22.5-acre winery in Napa Valley. Including all these, his wine factory in Napa valley also helps him make a fair sum of money throughout the year.

He graduated with B.Sc. in Business and MBA from California State University.

David Bakhtiari Siblings Are Also Into Sports

Among David’s siblings, two of his brothers are also into sports as football players.

His older brothers have a background in sports. Both of his brothers are into sports, majorly in football programs.

Bakhtiari is from a family of four siblings, three boys, and a girl. Thus, he has a sister and two brothers, Eric Bakhtiari, Danielle Bakhtiari, and Andrew Bakhtiari.

Oldest Brother Eric Played As A Linebacker

The oldest brother, Eric, played as a linebacker for the Tennessee Titans in 2009 and the San Francisco 49ers in 2012.

Eric was a two-time AP-All-American defensive end at the University of San Diego, where he played for Coach Jim Harbaugh.

Eventually, he played linebacker and special teams professionally for five years in the NFL. He ultimately ended his playing career with his hometown team, the San Francisco 49ers.

Eric recently moved to Chicago after working in Australia for the last few years.

His Brother Andrew Played As A Defensive

Similarly, his other older brother, Andrew Bakhtiari, played at the University of San Diego as a defensive end.

Andrew attended Junipero Serra HS, where he earned 2nd Team All-League and All-County honors. Bakhtiari was also named Most Valuable Offensive Lineman with a team going 7-3. While his junior year team scored 9-3, he also won the Western Catholic Athletic League title. 

All this information also confirms that the Bakhtiari family comprises die-hard fans of San Diego, as per the sources. We can also assume that David inspired himself to grow into sports from his family and elder brothers.

Corresponding to this, David grew to be involved in sports from a young age as he played and explored almost every sport, like soccer, baseball, lacrosse, and football but ended up sticking with football out of all.

David’s Sister Works As A Manager

As for his sister, Danielle Bakhtiari Barbic, she lives in San Diego, California, with her husband, Garett Barbic.

And she has been working at an innovative and expanding company named Houzz as a Lead Sales Manager.
