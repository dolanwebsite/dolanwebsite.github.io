Who Is Shaborn Emmanuel? Gregory Ross Murder 

Shaborn Emmanuel from Gregory Ross murder case was a drug dealer who supplied his suppliers with massive quantities of narcotics. 

Speaking of the case, Shaborn had dispatched a girlfriend to collect kilogram cocaine from the supplier. His girlfriend, however, was robbed of the narcotics before she could deliver them to him.

Following the incident, Emmanuel’s drug suppliers kidnapped his lover and kept her for ransom. The dealer had told him that he could only get her back if he paid for the missing narcotics or brought the head of whoever stole them.

Mr. Emmanuel was unaware of who had stolen the drugs. So he ended up driving to the Bronx and assassinated a destitute man named Gregory Ross.

Mr. Emmanuel, on the other hand, was already old and had grey hair when he was apprehended. He was 53 years old at the time, to be exact.

Was Shaborn Emmanuel Arrested?

After pleading guilty to murder, Shaborn Emmanuel got arrested in the year 2011. 

Shaborn was in jail in North Carolina for a drug offense even before the guilty plea to manslaughter. Later, Emmanuel was taken to New York City after authorities discovered evidence linking him to the murder.

Gregory Ross’s family was preparing for a trial. But the deceased guy would face one final indignation.
