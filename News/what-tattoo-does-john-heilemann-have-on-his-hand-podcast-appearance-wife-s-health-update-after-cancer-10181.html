What Tattoo Does John Heilemann Have On His Hand?

John Heilemann has a tattoo of the Aspen Freak Power Party badge, which is a six-fingered fist clutching a peyote button, on his hand.

According to Wikipedia, it refers to Hunter S. Thompson’s article The Battle of Aspen, which was featured in Rolling Stone No. 67 on October 1, 1970.

The title Freak Power in the Rockies appeared on the cover of the magazine, and the piece was eventually republished with that description in The Great Shark Hunt.

The topic of the essay is the 1969 mayoral election in Aspen, Colorado, and specifically Joe Edwards’ run as an attorney, motorcyclist, and non-conformist Aspen inhabitant.

It also outlines Thompson’s Freak Power program, which he would use the following year when campaigning for Sheriff of Pitkin County, Colorado.

Heilemann has always been fascinated by books and articles, and he has a number of other tattoos on his body as a tribute to a number of other wonderful works.
