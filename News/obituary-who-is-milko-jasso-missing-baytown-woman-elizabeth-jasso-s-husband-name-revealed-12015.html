Who Is Milko Jasso From Baytown?

Milko Jasso was a resident of Bayton.

He is the husband of recently missing Elizabeth Jasso.

He was 29 years old back in February 2021 when he was shot and killed.

Much information about his personal life has not come to the surface yet.

Nonetheless, Milko had a loving family and wife by his side through his good and bad days.

He has left his family in great pain, he will be missed by his family for the rest of their life.

Who Is Elizabeth Jasso From Baytown?

Elizabeth Jasso was a pregnant woman with twins from Baytown.

She has been missing from her home since August 5, 2021.

Jasso was due to deliver the two baby boys on the same day she disappeared.

Her in-laws and family are desperately trying to locate and find her after she went missing.

She was seen leaving her home in her white BMW car in surveillance footage from her neighbor’s CCTV camera.

Interestingly, Jasso was not carrying her hospital bag with her while she left her home.

Her family is claiming Elizabeth faked her pregnancy and she was not even able to become pregnant.

She was last seen at the grave of her husband and has disappeared ever since.

Police claim, she must have been suffering from the trauma of losing her husband at such young age.

Obituary: Was Milko Jasso Death A Murder?

Milko Jasso died on February 17, 2021.

He was shot at around 4:40 am according to the police records.

Milko allegedly tried to physically confront a 20-year-old woman at her house.

Then her 36-year-old boyfriend shot Milko to death.

Jasso was shot at least one time and died.

However, information about his death as an act of self-defense or murder has not been clear yet.

Nonetheless, Milko died leaving his wife in physiological trauma and his family heartbroken.
