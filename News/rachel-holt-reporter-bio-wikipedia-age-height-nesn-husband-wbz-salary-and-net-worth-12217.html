    Rachel Holt NESN

    Rachel is a host and reporter for New England Sports Network. At NESN, she covers some of the country’s most successful teams and players, including the Red Sox, Patriots, Bruins, and Celtics. Big events she has covered include Super Bowl LI, Super Bowl LIII, the 2017 NBA Draft, the 2018 NHL Awards, the 2019 Stanley Cup Final, and much more.

    You can find Rachel on NESN’s digital platforms, or anchoring NESN Sports Update on television. Prior to NESN, Rachel got her on-air start at a CBS-affiliate in Twin Falls, Idaho as an anchor/reporter. Often acting as a one-man-band, she cultivated her skills, including shooting, producing, editing, and reporting.

    A graduate of Northeastern University, Rachel is happy to be back in the city where she first discovered her passion for reporting while working for Northeastern University Athletics. She graduated magna cum laude with a B.A. in Communication Studies and a minor in Production.
