Is Kate Garner Leaving Fox 8?

Yes, Kate Garner is leaving FOX 8. Matter of fact, she has already left the channel, confirmed via her latest Twitter post.

Kate did her last show at FOX 8 back in November 2021. According to her, she remembers her time at the channel, “My time here will always be one of the happiest and most exciting of my career. I smile every day, and have memories to last my entire life!”

On her Instagram post on November 18, 2021, Garner confirmed the news of her departure to her followers and viewers.

The journalist worked for FOX 8 from Jun 2016 to November 2021. She joined the channel from WECT, leaving the job of the broadcast journalist to become a weather forecaster at FOX 8.

It was on Fox 8, she went on to get her Meteorology Certification from Penn State.
