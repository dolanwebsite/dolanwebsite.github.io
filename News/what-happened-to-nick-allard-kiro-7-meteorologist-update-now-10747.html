What Happened To Nick Allard From KIRO 7? Is He On Vacation?

Nick Allard has been missing from the screen lately, making fans wonder where is he now. He took Twitter on January 31, 2022, to disclose that he is taking some extended time off work to be at home, reports The Sun.

Allard revealed that getting up at 2 AM every day can be tiring and has impacted his overall health. He is taking some quality time off to recharge and spend some time with his family.

Nick might also be taking time off social media as he has not tweeted or posted anything since announcing that he is taking time off to recharge.

Allard is not on a vacation and is just taking some time away from work.

One of his fans tweeted that KIRO 7 morning news is not the same without Nick. He adds something extra to the weather reports, and they hope that the meteorologist comes back soon.

Meet Nick Allard Wife

Nick Allard and his wife Megan Allard live in Seattle, Washington D.C. The couple has two sons and one daughter. Nick’s favorite thing is to spend time with his wife and their three children.

Allard and his wife have been together for over 17 years. They first met while he was shadowing for a Seahawks game and she was supporting the Rams.

He likes staying connected to the community, and being real is his core value to him. He loves taking new technology and using them to enhance storytelling.
