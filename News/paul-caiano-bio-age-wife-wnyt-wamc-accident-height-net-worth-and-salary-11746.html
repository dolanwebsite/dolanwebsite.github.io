Paul Caiano Biography

Paul Caiano is an American journalist who works as a meteorologist for WNYT News Channel 13 which is an NBC-affiliated television station based in Albany, NY. He is also a weather reporter and anchor for WAMC which is a public radio network located in Albany, NY in the United States of America. Before joining WNYT, Paul worked as a paperboy just after college.

Besides, the meteorologist reports weather news at NewsChannel 13 Early Today and Today alongside Subrina Dhammi and Asa Stackel who are both news anchors. Furthermore, Paul earned the Presidential and Honors Convocation Awards in his junior year for research at the University of Albany on the correlation between cloud-to-ground lightning strikes and violent tornadoes. The weatherman is also the official meteorologist for Tanglewood Music Center in Lenox, Mass.
