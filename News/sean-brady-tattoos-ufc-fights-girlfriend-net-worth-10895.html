Sean Brady debuted in the Ultimate Fighting Championship (UFC) on October 19, 2019. And, he faced the UFC veteran Court McGee at UFC on ESPN 6.

Similarly, the fighter was expected to face Bela Muhammad on December 19, 2020. This fight was going to b held at UGC Fight Night.

However, in 2021 his fighting journey continued. Recently, on March 6, 2021, Sean Brady faced Jake Matthews at UFC 259. And he won the fight via submission in round three.
