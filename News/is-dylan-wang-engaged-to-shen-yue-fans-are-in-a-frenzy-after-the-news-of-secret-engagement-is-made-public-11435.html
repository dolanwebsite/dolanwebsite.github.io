Is Dylan Wang Engaged To Shen Yue?

No, Dylan Wang is not engaged with Shen Yue. Twitter is going crazy about the recent rumors circulating about the engagement of popular Chinese actor Dylan Wang. However, the gossips say that it is with his non-showbiz girlfriend.

It is still unknown whether the news about him exchanging rings is true or not but fans are wishing that his better half would be his costar Shen Yue. Fans have been shipping since they first appeared together.

There are a hundred speculations going on on social media but the true news is yet to come out. Until and unless a verifiable source does not come out with the actual happening we will have to keep on guessing.

Similarly, there aren’t any kind of suspected pictures available as well.

Dylan Wang Girlfriend

Dylan and Shen appeared in the ‘Meteor garden’ together for the first time on screen and were instantly the most loved couple on Television. There were fan pages created for them and people actually wanted to see them together.

Since then, Dylan’s girlfriend has been speculated to be Shen Yue. Even though they have never confirmed it, YouTubers and other media portals are busy collecting evidence to call them a secret couple.

Other than this, the actor has been extremely private about his love life and has never revealed his relationships from the past. Dylan is just 23 years old and it is understandable that the actor would want to focus on his career rather than datings.

Dylan Wang and Shen Yue Relationship Timeline

Dylan and Shen met on the sets of Meteor garden in 2018. That was Dylan’s first project on Television and Shen was already a known face by that time. However, after the release of the show, they became international sensations.

They have even appeared in a reality show called ‘Phanta City’ in 2017. As it is unknown if the two were ever together, even though they have been spotted together several times we cannot confirm the gossip.
