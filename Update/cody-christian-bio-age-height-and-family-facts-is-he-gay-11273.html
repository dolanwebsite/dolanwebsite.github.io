The name Cody Christian will certainly familiar to fans of the ABC Family series popularly known as Pretty Little Liars; Cody is best known for his recurring role as Mike Montgomery in the series. He is an upcoming actor of American origin who made his movie debut in the 2009 sci-fi film, Surrogates. Prior to his big screen debut, he was already on the small screen since 2007 as part of the cast of State of Mind – a comedy-drama television series. A lot of plaudits came his way when he joined the fifth and sixth seasons of the MTV series Teen Wolf, portraying the character of Theo Raeken.

Since his professional debut, Cody Christian has appeared in several big-screen and small-screen productions, notable among them are The Starving Games, Kill the Irishman, Submerged as well as Killing Animals. The young actor has come a long way, amassing a substantial fan following over the years. He is also known for his role in All-American – the CW Network’s new fall series where he was cast in the role of Asher, a high school footballer.

Cody Christian’s Bio (Age)

Cody Allen Christian was born in California, the United States on the 15th of April 1995. The young actor has not revealed any details on his academic qualifications and institutions attended but according to the records, he developed a healthy dose of interest in both bodybuilding and other sporting activities as an adolescent. However, the lure of the bright lights of the movie world stayed his sporting aspirations and he made the decision to relocate to Los Angeles in pursuit of an acting career.

The actor is a diehard fan of horror movies especially of classics like Halloween and Exorcist, which are his all-time-favorites. Since the inception of his career in entertainment, Cody Christian has recorded a great deal of success with his acting credits encompassing the likes of Grey’s Anatomy, True Blood and Back to You where he appeared alongside Laura Marano. He joined the cast of Kill the Irish Man in 2011 and played one of the leading roles in The Starving Games which was released in 2013 as a spoof of The Hunger Games movie.

Away from the acting world, Cody Christian is also a competent rapper, who has collaborated with Kenny Kynoch to cover the single Fragile, a song originally from Tech Ngne. He is an animal lover with a special liking for cats, always posing on Instagram with his feline friend – Athena, which currently lives with him in LA California.

Family Facts

Unlike details about most of his early life, details about Cody Christian’s family are all out in the open. His father, who is Caucasian, has been identified as Scott Bohall, a veteran in the army while his mother, Ashley Bohall, is a Native American. Cody is not an only child; he spent his formative years on a reservation located in Maine alongside an older brother called Joshua Christian.

The actor’s mother is a breast cancer survivor, while she was battling with the dreadful ailment which was diagnosed in 2014, Cody Christian staged a fundraising campaign towards breast cancer research. He seems to share a very strong bond with his mum as he is always seen assisting her in the kitchen. On her own part, Ashley has shared beautiful photos of Cody as a baby on Twitter.

Is Cody Christian Gay?

Although rumors ran rife about his sexual orientation when his private videos leaked, Cody Christian is not gay as he has dated a good number of the girls in the past. He was an item with Talia Maxine who works as an actress and a fitness instructor, the duo was always spotted together at several functions and even holidayed together in Disneyland in August 2015. They decided to call it quits in 2016 but the reason for their separation was never revealed. Later in 2017, he got together with a model, Alex Swift and they are still together till date, but before Alex, his name was linked with Bella Thorne earlier in 2017.

Height

Considering his love for sports and bodybuilding, it is not surprising to hear that Cody Christian has an athletic build. He stands at a height of 5 feet 8 inches which goes down well with his body weight of 72kg. Cody Christian also has dark brown hair and blue eyes.
