Sophie Alakija is a Nigerian actress and model who is a Lebanese and Efik descent. Sophia Alakija was born on  7th February 1985 in Lagos, Nigeria. Sophie is very popular because of the roles she played in Halit and Assistant Madams movies which went viral in Nigeria and Africa.

Sophie Alakija started her career when she featured in the Holla At Your boy music video of Wizkid, a Nigerian music superstar. Sophie has also acted so many movies such as Getting Over him, Drawing Strands, and Small Chops which are all major hit movies in Nigeria.

Sophie Alakija also featured in a TV series called Scandals in 2017. The Scandal series is a collaboration movie between Ghanaian and Nigerians in which Sophie played one of the major roles.
