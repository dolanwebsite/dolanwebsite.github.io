Amy Duncan Biography; Net Worth, Age, Artist And Height Of Tim Duncan’s Ex-Wife

Amy Duncan is the ex-wife of NBA star Tim Duncan. Amy became popular after her marriage to NBA star Tim Duncan who is presently the coach and former player of the San Antonio Spurs.

Amy was born in 1977 in North Carolina to Fred and Judy Sherrill. She attended Wake Forest University, Winston-Salem in North Carolina where she met Tim Duncan. She was a cheerleader then for a while. Her friendship with Tim grew and they started dating in 1992. They later got married after nine years in 2001 amidst her fears of being left alone at home while Tim traveled around playing basketball. They had their children Sydney and Draven Duncan in 2005 and 2007 respectively.

After 12 years, the marriage fell on the rocks and the two opted for a divorce which they got in 2013. Amy stated that their relationship had become insupportable due to conflicts.
