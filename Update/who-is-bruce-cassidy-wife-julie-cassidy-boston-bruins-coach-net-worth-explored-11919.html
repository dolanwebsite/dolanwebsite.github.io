Boston Bruins head coach, Bruce Cassidy is married to his gorgeous wife Julie Cassidy. Here is everything you need to know about the coach’s relationship and family members. 

Bruce Cassidy is a hockey coach and former player from Canada, who played for the Chicago Blackhawks in the NHL.

The former player was selected by Chicago Black Hawks of NHL in the 1983 NHL Entry Draft in first round overall 18th pick. Bruce retired as a player and became the head coach of the Jacksonville Lizard Kings of the ECHL.

Being a head coach, he served in different clubs and organizations which include, Trenton Titans, Indianapolis Ice, Kingston Frontenacs, according to icehockey. fandom.

Further, he served as an assistant coach at the Boston Bruins for the season 2016-17 season.

Later, on April 26, Cassidy was named head coach of the team. He is about to represent Canada as an assistant hockey coach at the upcoming 2022 Winter Olympic Games as per WCVB.
