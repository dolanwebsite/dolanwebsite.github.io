Precious Way is an actress from Chicago, USA. Aside from that, she is also a rapper and social media personality. The actress is widely known for appearing in the soap opera Days of Our Lives. She was also in the American musical drama Tv show Queens.

Currently, Way is in the media as she is in the new Tv series Partners In Rhyme with rapper and DJ Mc Lyte. The show is airing this Thursday, November 18, only on ALLBLK.

Way plays the character of Luscious T in the series. The show is about the rising female high school rapper and social media star Luscious who believes that she is the best spitter in the rap game since Cardi B.

In 2018, Precious released her first single titled Watch Me Go. The song did decently well, allowing her to play on tours and music festivals in places like Times Square and Disney World.
