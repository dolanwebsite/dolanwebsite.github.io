Abu Abel Egbarin, known professionally as Abu Abel, is a renowned Nigerian entrepreneur, talent manager, and the CEO of Obimzy Record Label. Abu Abel Profile

Educational Background

Abu Abel’s educational journey includes attending the Army Children School, Sagamu, and Sonyindo Community High School. He furthered his education by graduating from Olabisi Onabanjo University.

Career

Abu Abel has built a successful career as a businessman, with investments in various sectors. He is known for his ventures in real estate, oil and gas, and the entertainment industry, both in Nigeria and internationally. He gained significant recognition for establishing the Obimzy Records label, through which he has worked with prominent artists such as Naira Marley, Slimcase, Zlatan, Olamide, Reminisce, Otunba Cash, and more.

As a talent manager, Abu Abel is often considered the driving force behind the success of many top musicians. He has a reputation for nurturing new talent and helping them advance in their careers.

Controversies

Abu Abel found himself in the midst of controversy when he was accused of allegedly beating up DJ Chicken. Following the viral videos depicting the incident, numerous Nigerians expressed their outrage on Abu Abel’s social media page. Many voiced their displeasure with the socialite and called for his arrest. This incident generated significant attention and debate among the public.
