Who Was Sammy The Bull Gravano Friend Louis Milito? Murder Case Update 

Louis has been reported to have been murdered by John Carneglia. Carneglia shot Louis on the head from the back after he was having his coffee. After Milito fell to the ground, John shot him again under the chin.

However, Milito’s wife, Linda, claims that her husband was murdered by Louis, which was informed by Gambino’s members. She also claims that Sammy gave her $5,000 and told her never to contact him. 

Nonetheless, this is not accurate as per Sammy because he denies any involvement in Louis’s death. He denies Linda’s claim about him breaking all contact and he claims that he was in contact with her after her husband’s died.

Louis Milito Wikipedia – Who Was He?

Louis Milito was a solider of Gambino crime family. His real name was Liborio “Louie” Milito. He was a childhood friend with another notorious gangster Sammy Gravano. They together started their criminal journey.

Milito does not have a dedicated Wikipedia. He and Sammy were together in the Rampers, a local street gang. They were involved in various criminal activities like car theft in that gang.

Later, Louis and other members of the Rampers joined the crime family, where they started to thrive more in criminal activities. However, for most of them, it did not go well.
