From a Chateau owner to a renowned Youtuber. Here is what you need to know about Stephanie Jarvis.

Stephanie Jarvis grew up in a spacious home in England after her British father and French mother purchased a vast country estate and converted it into an Alzheimer’s care facility.

Her parents, both nurses, thought that living in a lovely environment would benefit the patients. As a result, the Jarvis family moved into the attic, and the patients were placed in the main house downstairs.

Jarvis, then 29 and half-heartedly pursuing a career in opera, realized she missed living in a large, pleasant home surrounded by people after a few years. So she decided to pool her resources with her ex, Nic, and bought a chateau with him.
