Alex Antetokounmpo Biography

Alex Antetokounmpo is a Greek professional basketball player for UCAM Murcia of the Spanish Liga ACB who was born on 27 August 2001 in Athens, Greece. Alex plays the small forward position. He is an alumnus of Dominican High School in Whitefish Bay, Wisconsin.

    Alex Basketballer

    Alex signed a three-year contract with UCAM Murcia of the Liga ACB on 22 June 2020 and on 18 October, he made his debut for the club’s reserve team in the Liga EBA, recording 28 points and six rebounds, shooting 6-of-9 from three-point range, in an 83–69 win over SCD Carolinas.

    Alex joined the Sacramento Kings for the 2021 NBA Summer League after going undrafted in the 2021 NBA draft. The Sacramento Kings then proceeded to win the 2021 NBA Summer League.

    Alex also played basketball for Dominican High School in Whitefish Bay for four years. As a freshman, he averaged 2.9 points per game he was a two-time all-state selection and averaged 20 points and seven rebounds per game as a senior. He obtained scholarship offers from DePaul, Ohio, and Green Bay. Alex reported on 9 May 2020 that he would play in Europe after graduating from high school, rather than playing college basketball. Alex was considered a three-star recruit by major recruiting service
