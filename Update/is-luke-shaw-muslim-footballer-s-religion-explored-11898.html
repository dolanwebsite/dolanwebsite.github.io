Yes, Luke Shaw is Muslim. Fans are curious about England left back’s religion after seeing photos of him celebrating Ramadan. Let’s find out more in this article.

Luke Shaw is an English professional footballer who plays for Manchester United in the English Premier League. He plays as a left-back for both Manchester and England’s squad.

Shaw has been a key player for England’s road to the finals of the 2020 Euro Cup. He has been in excellent form for the three lions and has assisted three assists in five matches played.

Is Luke Shaw Muslim?

Yes, Luke Shaw is Muslim by religion.

Shaw was photographed in Dubai in 2017 celebrating Ramadan along with other players like Adnan Januzaj and Bojan. All of them could be seen wearing religious attire in the pictures.

Luke is one of many players who have Muslim faith. Zinedine Zidane, Mesut Ozil, and Paul Pobga are just some of them.

Talking about his playing style, Luke Shaw has an attacking style of football despite playing as a left-back. He has been compared with young Gareth Bale as he played the same position.

We can find Luke on Wikipedia. He is also available on Instagram under the username @lukeshaw23. He has 3.1 million followers on the platform.

Did Luke Shaw Convert His Religion?

Many people have speculated that Luke Shaw recently converted his religion to Muslim. However, he has not spoken about it and we don’t know exactly when he did it, and remains a mystery.

Luke was born on July 12, 1995, to Paul and Joanna Shaw. He was born and raised in Kingston upon Thames, London. He was a Chelsea supporter while growing up and was even in their development center.

There is no information about his parents’ religious beliefs. So, we really don’t know when he converted or which religion he previously followed. We cannot confirm anything until Luke shares about them.
