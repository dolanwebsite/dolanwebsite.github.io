Veronica Webb Biography

Veronica Webb is an American model, actress, writer, and television personality and the first African American to have a major cosmetics contract. She has appeared on covers of Vogue, Essence and Elle magazines and on the runway for Victoria’s Secret and Chanel.

Webb was born in Detroit, Michigan and as a youth, she identified with magazine models and women who were involved in the fashion industry, who exhibited control over their appearance. Veronica also enjoyed comic books and dreamed of becoming an animator.

Veronica Webb Education

Webb graduated from the Detroit Waldorf School in 1983 and moved to New York to study design. She later dropped out of school to become a model after a makeup artist saw her in a housewares boutique.

Veronica Webb Career

Webb began her modelling career after she dropped out of school in New York where she was studying design. In 1989, Webb appeared in the music video for New Order’s “Round and Round”. Also in 1985, she appears briefly in Scritti Politti’s “Perfect Way” video.

Webb would later become a spokesmodel for Revlon, becoming the first African-American to win an exclusive contract for a major cosmetics company.

She made her feature film debut in Spike Lee’s Jungle Fever in 1991. Some of her film credits include Malcolm X, Someone Like You and The Big Tease. She also appeared in television show. Some television shows she appeared in are like Damon, Just Shoot Me! Clueless and Becker.

When working as a runway model, Webb modelled fashions from the collections of Azzedine Alaia, Isaac Mizrahi, Karl Lagerfeld, and Todd Oldham. She was named to American Vogue’s best dressed list three times.

Veronica Webb Age

Veronica is 54 years old as of 2019. She was born on February 25, 1965.

Veronica Webb Family

Parents

Veronica is the daughter of Marion Stewart and Leonard Douglas Webb. Her mother was a public health nurse while her father was an electrician. Before her birth, her parents had spent 20 years in the Army. Veronica grew up in a working-class community. One of her sisters is an oncologist while the other is a mathematician.

Veronica Webb George Robb

In the year 2002, Veronica married George Robb. The couple was blessed with two daughters, Leila Rose Robb who was born in October 2002 and Molly Blue Robb who was born in May 2004. Veronica and Rob however divorced in 2009.

Chris Delgatto Veronica Webb

After her divorce with George Rob, Veronica began dating Chris Delgatto, a jewelry expert in 2013. They are now married. Chris has two children from his previous marriage; Leonard and Alessandra.

Veronica Webb Daughters

Webb has two children; daughters, from her previous marriage to George Robb. Her firstborn daughter is Leila Rose Robb who was born in October 2002 and the second and last is Molly Blue Robb who was born in May 2004.
