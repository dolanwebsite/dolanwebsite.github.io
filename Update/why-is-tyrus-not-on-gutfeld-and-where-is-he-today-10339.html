Why Is Tyrus Not On Gutfeld And Where Is He Today?

 Posted on November 3, 2023  |  3 minutes  |  477 words  |  Enda Jantzen

George Murdoch, also known as Tyrus, is a 49-year-old wrestler from Boston, Massachusetts, the United States. He is now the National Wrestling Alliance’s World TV Champion.

In the past, he had competed for WWE as Brodus Clay. He is also a cable news personality known for co-hosting the late-night show Gutfeld! on Fox News Channel.

On the October 15 show of Impact Wrestling in 2014, he faced Shark Boy and won his first bout in professional wrestling with the ring name Tyrus.

Fox News: Why Is Tyrus Not On Gutfeld?

Tyrus has been absent from Gutfeld for the past several days. And his supporters are badly missing him on the program.

Many are curious to learn about his sudden disappearance from the show. Meanwhile, some suspect that he has any issues with Fox News.

We assure everyone that none of those rumors are accurate. He is still in close contact with the channel.

So far now, Tyrus hasn’t made any comments regarding this matter. His busy schedule could be the reason he skipped the show.

Where Is Tyrus Today?

WWE champion Tyrus published an autobiography called Just Tyrus: A Memoir in 2022, which has gained prominence as a New York Times bestseller.

Through his social media accounts, he announced the launch of his book. Additionally, he posted a small glimpse of this audiobook.

Also, he is now competing at the National Wrestling Alliance (NWA). In March, he signed the agreement with the professional wrestling promotion and made his NWA debut at Back For The Attack.

He won the NWA World Television Championship on the August 6 episode of NWA Extra Power by defeating The Pope.

From Thursday, the tickets for his show go on sale as he, alongside Kat Timpf, will be presenting At New Jersey Performing Arts Centers (NJPAC) on September 23.

Is Tyrus Still On The Gutfeld Show?

Even though Tyrus, the frequent panelist on The Great Gutfeld Show on Fox News, hasn’t appeared recently, he is still on the show.

Hopefully, he will appear on the program soon and delight the viewers with his incredible sense of humor.

Since he hasn’t made any announcement about leaving the program, he is supposedly on a break for a while.

He just posted old Gutfeld! Video to his Instagram account. Therefore, it is inevitable that he is indeed connected to Fox News.

At first, host Gutfeld invited him to use the stage name “Tyrus” and appear twice a month on the program.

He then started regularly contributing to The Daily Briefing, the Fox News host Dana Perino’s old daytime news program.

For six years, Tyrus has been a guest analyst on The Great Gutfeld Show. With his incredible charisma, he has gained a sizable fan base.
