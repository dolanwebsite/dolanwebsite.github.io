Who Are Michele Thornton Ghee Parents?

Michele Thornton Ghee was born to African American parents, on 26 June 1966, in Oakland, California.

Unfortunately, information on her mother is scarce on the internet. However, Michele’s father, Walt Thorton, was the one who had a major impact on her life.

Walt, her late dad, was a community activist and an entrepreneur. He used to own a restaurant in the Bay Area. Sadly, at just the age of 51, Mr. Thorton passed away following complications from cancer.

Seeing her father lying on his deathbed, Ghee made a promise to herself to turn her life around and make him proud. Then, she focused on her education and obtained her Bachelor of Arts at the age of thirty, in May of 2007.
