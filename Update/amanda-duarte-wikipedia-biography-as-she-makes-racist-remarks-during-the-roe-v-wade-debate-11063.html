Dead Darlings Host Amanda Duarte Wikipedia & Biography

Dead Darlings host Amanda Duarte does not have a Wikipedia profile for her biography yet.

However, we have covered everything to know about her in this article for our readers and viewers and tried to draw her profile.

She is the founder of Dead Darlings, she came up with the concept four years ago while struggling with a new play and musing on the adage that writers often have to “kill their darlings” as part of the process according to The New York Times.

Ms. Duarte suggested the concept to her friend Micah Bucey, 36, who is the pastor of the arts at Judson, a Greenwich Village church whose purpose includes encouraging creativity.

Amanda started her career as a regional theater actor and later moved to New York City to become a Broadway actor but Big Apple had other plans for her according to The Creative Independent.

She ditched the idea of being an actor after she was not satisfied with herself and took up writing, she started as a storyteller and later landed jobs in several publications as a writer.
