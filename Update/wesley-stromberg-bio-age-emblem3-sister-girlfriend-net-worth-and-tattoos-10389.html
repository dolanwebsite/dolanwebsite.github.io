    Emblem3 Wesley Stromberg

    Emblem3 is an American reggae pop band from Sequim, Washington. The band consists of Wesley Stromberg, Drew Chadwick, and Keaton Stromberg. He is currently working on a solo album, while the band is currently on hiatus. He was previously a member of various bands before forming Emblem3.

    The band initially auditioned for America’s Got Talent but dropped out at the last minute. They later auditioned for The X Factor (USA) after receiving a call notifying them to audition. They auditioned using their original song Sunset Blvd.

    They got through the audition and finish 4th on the show. The show gave the band worldwide recognition and an opportunity to share their music with the world.
