Thomas Costello Missing Case – What happened in Dulce Base?

Thomas Case has been missing for more than four decades now. He and his whole family, went missing overnight after Costello published a picture from the security footage.

He was working as a security guard in Dulce Base, based in New Mexico, USA. It was in this very place where it has been conspired to be the new Area 51. As the conspiracy goes, a joined facility of humans and aliens, the underground facility holds the secret to extraterrestrial life.

This conspiracy began to fly around after a State trooper spotted a strange craft in the sky and butchered cattle in the ground in Dulce, Mexico. However, there has been no concrete evidence of the statement being true.

The picture that Thomas Costello supposedly published turned out to be a hoax. As revealed by The Black Vault Case Files, the picture that has been circulating on the web is a still from a movie.

The question still remains the same after all these. If those pictures were indeed hoaxes, where exactly are Thomas Costello and his family?
