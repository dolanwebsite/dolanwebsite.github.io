Chris Elliott Movies and TV Shows

Elliot appeared in numerous films and TV shows including “Cabin Boy,” “There’s Something About Mary,” “Scary Movie 2,” “Groundhog Day,” “How I Met Your Mother,” “Parks and Recreation,” and “Brooklyn Nine-Nine.”

Elliot also appeared on the 90s sketch comedy show “Saturday Night Live”, “Friends From College”, “According To Jim’, “The Naked Truth”, “Schitt’s Creek”, and many more.

Chris Elliott Wikipedia

Chris Elliott is an award-winning actor who starred in the sitcom pilot of Maggie in 2021, and has appeared in many other sitcoms including How I Met Your Mother” from 2009 to 2014.

His  comedic sketches on the late night talk show “Late Night With David Letterman” during the 80s had shot him to fame.

Aside from acting, Elliot explores other areas of arts and entertainment such as comedy, directing, and writing.

The Emmy Award winner has made notable appearances in many TV series including Everybody Loves Raymond, How I Met Your Mother, Adult Swim’s Eagleheart, and Schitt’s Creek.

In terms of feature films, Elliot starred in Groundhog Day, There’s Something About Mary, Snow Day, and Scary Movie 2.

In 2015, Elliot played a guest role in the crime series Law & Order: Special Victims Unit. The following year, he was cast in the Sony Crackle series Thanksgiving.
